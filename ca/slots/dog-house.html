How The Dog House works

The Dog House is a 5x3 grid slot from Pragmatic Play on 20 fixed paylines. It is one of Pragmatic Play's most widely distributed titles and the original entry in the Dog House series that later spawned The Dog House Megaways and other variants.

The symbol set uses a dog kennel theme: kennel, paw print, dog bowl, and toy as low-pays; four dog breeds (Great Dane, Pug, Rottweiler, German Shepherd) as premiums. The German Shepherd is the highest-paying regular symbol. Wild symbols substitute for all regular symbols and include a multiplier: when a wild contributes to a line win, the win is multiplied by 3x.

Scatter symbols (the dog kennel with money) trigger the girs gratuïts feature. The 20-line payline structure uses standard left-to-right win formation with pays for 3 or more matching symbols starting from reel 1.

Multiple wilds on the same win with 3x multipliers each multiply together: two wilds on the same line produce a 9x multiplier on the line win. Three wilds on the same line produce 27x. This multiplicative wild system creates the base game's variance moments despite the standard 5x3, 20-line structure.

Bonus features

Three scatter symbols trigger 10 girs gratuïts; four scatters award 15 girs gratuïts; five scatters award 20 girs gratuïts. During girs gratuïts, wild symbols are sticky: when a wild lands, it freezes in position for the remainder of the girs gratuïts session. Additional wilds landing during subsequent girs gratuïts also become sticky.

As sticky wilds accumulate across the reels over the girs gratuïts session, later spins benefit from an increasing number of pre-placed wilds. Each sticky wild retains its 3x multiplier. When multiple sticky wilds combine on the same payline win during a later free spin, their multipliers multiply together.

Free spins can be retriggered by landing additional scatters during the feature. Bonus buy at 100x stake provides direct girs gratuïts access. The 6,750x max win ceiling requires a girs gratuïts session where sticky wilds accumulate across multiple reels and the top premium symbols land on multiple lines simultaneously under the combined multiplier.
