Турнири and competitions covered

Stake follows professional tennis from the first ATP250 of January through to the Davis Cup and BJK Cup finals in Неvember.

  • Australian Open - The first Grand Slam of the year, played on hard courts at Melbourne Park in January. Jannik Sinner won the 2024 and 2025 editions, cementing his status as the season-opening favourite heading into 2026. Stake lists full draw markets from the moment the bracket is released.
  • Roland Garros (French Open) - Clay's most prestigious event runs May-June in Paris. Rafael Nadal's 14 titles shaped the narrative for a generation; now Carlos Alcaraz and Sinner contest the clay-court throne. Iga Swiatek's record on clay makes her the consistent outright selection for the women's draw.
  • Wimbledon - The grass-court major played at the All England Club in late June and July is the sport's most iconic tournament. Djokovic's seven titles and the women's draw volatility on fast grass create a unique betting environment. Stake covers every match from qualifying through the final.
  • US Open - New York's hard-court major in August-September closes the Grand Slam calendar. The open draw and the physical demands of the American summer produce frequent upsets, rewarding in-play bettors who track fitness and serving percentages in real time.
  • ATP Masters 1000 - Nine elite events sit directly below the Slams: Indian Wells, Miami, Monte Carlo, Madrid, Rome, Canada, Cincinnati, Shanghai and Paris-Bercy. Masters titles count heavily in the race for the ATP Finals and attract the strongest fields outside the four Slams.
  • ATP Finals - The season-ending championship in Turin brings together the eight highest-ranked singles and doubles players of the year. A round-robin group stage followed by knockouts provides an unusual betting format that Stake covers comprehensively.
  • WTA Tour - Every WTA 1000 and WTA 500 tournament is listed on Stake alongside the Slams. Aryna Sabalenka, Coco Gauff and Swiatek lead the standings through the 2025-26 cycle.
  • ATP Challenger and ITF - Lower-tier events give punters a chance to identify emerging talent before they break into the top 50. Stake lists match-winner markets for Challenger fixtures on most circuits.
  • Davis Cup - The 100-year-old national team competition now uses a Finals format in Malaga each Неvember. Teams of three singles players and a doubles pair compete across ties, with outright and tie-winner markets available.
  • Billie Jean King Cup (BJK Cup) - The women's equivalent of the Davis Cup, also using a Finals format. Stake covers the qualifying rounds and the Finals in full.

Most popular tennis betting markets

  • Match winner - Back either player to win the match. The foundation market, available from qualifying to finals on every tour event.
  • Set winner - Bet on which player wins a specific set (Set 1, Set 2, etc.) rather than the overall match. Useful in-play as momentum shifts after a tight set.
  • Set betting (correct set score) - Predict the exact scoreline in sets: 2-0, 2-1 for best-of-three; 3-0, 3-1, 3-2 for Grand Slams. Enhanced коефициенти reward precise forecasting.
  • Total games (over/under) - Will the total number of games played exceed or fall below a stated line? A competitive match between Djokovic and Alcaraz often goes deep into a deciding set, pushing totals above 40 games.
  • Total sets - Will the match be decided in 2 sets or go to 3 (best-of-three), or reach 4 or 5 in a Grand Slam? Pre-match form data on sets won drives this market.
  • Handicap (game handicap) - One player is given a game advantage or disadvantage. If Djokovic is -4.5 games, he needs to win by 5 or more net games across the match.
  • To win first set and match - A double-result combination popular with accumulators. Taking the more likely set winner and match winner at combined коефициенти adds value versus two separate markets.

In-play and live tennis betting

Тенис is one of the best sports for live betting because the score resets at the start of every game, creating constant коефициенти movement. Stake's in-play tennis interface updates within seconds of each point, letting you react to a break of serve, a mid-match injury timeout or a sudden shift in momentum that is visible before the bookmaker recalibrates fully.

Pre-match research is essential: surface win rate, head-to-head record, recent form on the tour and serving statistics (first serve percentage, aces per match, double faults) all inform a well-placed pre-match selection. In-play betting, however, is where tennis delivers its greatest edge. When Djokovic loses the first set at Wimbledon, live коефициенти on his recovery often over-correct relative to his career comeback rate, presenting value for the in-play bettor who knows his historical second-set records.

Key in-play markets at Stake include: next game winner, current set winner, next set score, number of aces in the next game and whether the current game will go to deuce. Select ATP and WTA matches are live-streamed on the platform.
