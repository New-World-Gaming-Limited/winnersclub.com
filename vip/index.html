The four recurring rewards

1. Level-up bonus

A one-time cash bonus paid every time you reach a new tier. Scales with the threshold, so the Platinum III level-up dwarfs the Bronze one. No wagering required to withdraw, in many cases.

2. Rakeback

A percentage of your house edge contribution returned to you continuously. The percentage scales with tier. At higher Diamond tiers it's a meaningful regular income for grinders who play volume.

3. Reload bonuses

Periodic top-up offers (weekly, then daily at higher tiers). A percentage match on deposit, usually with a modest wagering attached, often lower than the welcome MAXBET 40× requirement.

4. Monthly bonus drop

A larger discretionary bonus issued by your host. Sized to your play volume and timed around your activity, not the calendar.

From Platinum I upward you're assigned a dedicated host. Practically, that means a single named point of contact in Stake's VIP team. Read the small print

Wager thresholds are turnover, not net loss. You can hit Bronze in a single Crash session with a small bankroll if you wager and re-wager. Originals at 99% RTP contribute 100% to turnover. Most slot bets contribute 100%. Live-dealer and table games are sometimes weighted lower, check the in-platform contribution chart for the current weighting.

Tier itself is permanent: once you hit Gold, you stay at Gold even if you stop playing. The recurring rewards (rakeback, reloads, monthly bonus) require ongoing activity. The level-up bonuses are one-time.
