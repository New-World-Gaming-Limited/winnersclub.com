الحدود والمواعيد

RBI removed the NEFT per-transaction ceiling in December 2019, meaning there is no cap on individual NEFT transfers at the network level. However, your bank may still impose daily transfer limits through net banking (common caps are 5 lakh to 25 lakh يومياً depending on the bank and account type). Check your bank's net banking settings under "Transaction Limits" to see your specific cap.

NEFT operates 24/7 on all days of the year including Sundays and public holidays since December 2019. Batches settle every 30 minutes, starting at midnight. If you initiate a transfer at 2:15 PM, the batch settles at 2:30 PM and Stake should receive the funds by 3:00 PM at the أحدث.
