How you can bet

Every modern wager type, plus a few worth reading the small print on.

Singles & Parlays

Standard moneylines, spreads, totals. Parlays stack legs across different events. Round robins and system bets build combinations automatically from multiple selections.

Same-Game Multi (SGM)

Stake's bet builder across markets within one event. Three types: Statistic (static props), H2H (player vs. player), Combined (ranking multiple players). Important: SGM is pre-match only, it can't be placed live, and cash-out doesn't apply to SGM or player props.

Accumulator Bonus

Winning accumulators with 5 or more selections earn up to a 50% bonus on the winnings. Stack five legs, win, collect 1.5× what standard odds would pay. Works across sports, not just football.

Asian Handicap

Full Asian handicap menu in all variations: quarter-ball, half-ball, whole-ball. Removes the draw from the equation on football and levels the field on lopsided fixtures. Essential for serious football bettors.

Cash-Out & Edit Bet

Full and partial cash-out on eligible pre-match and live bets. Lock in a return or cut losses before the whistle. Odds update near-instantly in-play, so fast fingers matter. Edit bet allows adding or removing legs on open parlays.

Futures & Outrights

Season-long markets: league winners, tournament outright, top scorer, relegation. Available well in advance with competitive margins on the big competitions.
