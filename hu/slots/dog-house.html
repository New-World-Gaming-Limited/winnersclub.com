Hogyan működik a The Dog House

The Dog House is a 5x3 grid slot from Pragmatic Play on 20 fixed paylines. It is one of Pragmatic Play's most widely distributed titles and the original entry in the Dog House series that later spawned The Dog House Megaways and other variants.

The symbol set uses a dog kennel theme: kennel, paw print, dog bowl, and toy as low-pays; four dog breeds (Great Dane, Pug, Rottweiler, German Shepherd) as premiums. The German Shepherd is the highest-paying regular symbol. Wild symbols substitute for all regular symbols and include a szorzó: when a wild contributes to a line win, the win is multiplied by 3x.

Scatter symbols (the dog kennel with money) trigger the ingyenes pörgetések feature. The 20-line payline structure uses standard left-to-right win formation with pays for 3 or more mérkőzésing symbols starting from reel 1.

Multiple wilds on the same win with 3x szorzós each multiply together: two wilds on the same line produce a 9x szorzó on the line win. Three wilds on the same line produce 27x. This multiplicative wild system creates the base játék's variance moments despite the standard 5x3, 20-line structure.

Bonus features

Three scatter symbols trigger 10 ingyenes pörgetések; four scatters award 15 ingyenes pörgetések; five scatters award 20 ingyenes pörgetések. During ingyenes pörgetések, wild symbols are sticky: when a wild lands, it freezes in position for the remainder of the ingyenes pörgetések session. Additional wilds landing during subsequent ingyenes pörgetések also become sticky.

As sticky wilds accumulate across the reels over the ingyenes pörgetések session, later spins benefit from an increasing number of pre-placed wilds. Each sticky wild retains its 3x szorzó. When multiple sticky wilds combine on the same payline win during a later free spin, their szorzós multiply together.

Free spins can be retriggered by landing additional scatters during the feature. Bonus buy at 100x tét provides direct ingyenes pörgetések access.
