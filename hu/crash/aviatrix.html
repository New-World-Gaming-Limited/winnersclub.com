Hogyan működik az Aviatrix

Aviatrix follows the established crash-játék format: a plane appears, a szorzó counter begins at 1.00x and rises, and you must cashout before the plane flies away. The játék ends azonnal when the plane disappears, and any uncashed bets are lost. The distinguishing feature of Aviatrix is the social layer built on top of this core mechanic.

A live rangsor displays the top cashouts achieved by all active játékosok in the current session. A shared bet feed shows every bet placed and every cashout executed in real time, which gives a sense of community activity without revealing strategic information about upcoming crash points.

NFT plane skins allow you to customise the aircraft with unique visual identifiers. These are purely cosmetic: the RNG determines the crash point independently of which plane skin is active. Skins can be acquired, traded, or held as blockchain assets outside the játék, but their presence or absence has no effect on your expected value.

Each round uses a bizonyíthatóan fair system. The server seed hash is publicly shown before betting opens. After the round, the seed is revealed and can be combined with your client seed in a publicly documented algorithm to reproduce the crash point. Avoiding social-proof bias: The public bet feed shows large wins prominently. This is a display feature, not a signal about upcoming rounds. Resist the temptation to increase your tét or cashout target based on what other játékosok are doing. Each round is independent.

The valószínűség of hitting 100x in a single session with a reasonable number of rounds is low; treat it as add megtainment expenditure rather than an income strategy.
