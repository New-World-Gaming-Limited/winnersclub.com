How to befizetés with JazzCash at Stake

How to withdraw from Stake to JazzCash

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Submit CNIC details and a selfie in the Profile ellenőrzés section before the first kifizetés.
  2. Open Cashier and válaszd ki Kifizetés. Choose JazzCash.
  3. Add Meg your JazzCash registered number. Format: 03XX-XXXXXXX. This must be a number with an active JazzCash mobil fiókot.
  4. Add Meg kifizetés amount and erősítsd meg details. The recipient name shown should mérkőzés your Stake regisztráció name.
  5. Submit. Stake debits your wallet and dispatches the transfer. JazzCash credits your mobil fiókot within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS erősítsd megs receipt.

Limitek és határidők

JazzCash mobil fiókot limits depend on your KYC level. Level 0 (unellenőrzött, registered with mobil number only): maximum wallet balance PKR 25,000, napi transactions PKR 25,000. Level 1 (CNIC ellenőrzött): maximum wallet balance PKR 200,000, napi transactions PKR 200,000. Level 2 (biometric ellenőrzés at Jazz franchise): PKR 500,000 per month limit.

To maximise your befizetés and kifizetés limits at Stake via JazzCash, fejezd be Level 1 ellenőrzés by látogasd meging any Jazz franchise with your CNIC. The process takes about 10 minutes and limits increase immediately after biometric regisztráció.

JazzCash has 99%+ uptime and operates 24/7. Befizetések are real-time erősítsd meged. Kifizetések from Stake depend on the payment queue; most process within the first hour during business hours (9 AM to 10 PM PKT).

Ország availability and pénznem

JazzCash is elérhető to Pakistani residents with a Jazz or Warid SIM. As of 2024, JazzCash has over 25 million registered fiókots and 130,000+ franchise points across Pakistan. All transactions are in PKR. JazzCash is regulated by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) as a Branchless Banking licence holder.

Non-Jazz SIM holders can also register for JazzCash using any Pakistani mobil number, though the wallet is primarily linked to Jazz/Warid numbers. Check JazzCash's weboldalt for non-Jazz regisztráció options.
