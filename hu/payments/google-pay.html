How to befizetés using Google Pay at Stake

  1. Log in to your Stake fiókot. New users: register first and add meg a promóciós kódot NEWBONUS.
  2. Click Befizetés in the cashier. The fizetési mód panel opens. Válaszd Ki Google Pay or the generic UPI option.
  3. Add Meg the befizetés amount. Minimum is 300 INR. The screen will display a QR code or UPI payment link.
  4. Open Google Pay on your phone. Tap "Scan QR code," point at the screen, or tap the UPI deep-link on mobil to jump directly into GPay. Erősítsd Meg the amount and approve with your UPI PIN.
  5. Check your Stake wallet. Funds appear within 60 seconds. If not, note the GPay transaction ID (visible in GPay under transaction history) and contact Stake live-chat support.

How to withdraw to Google Pay from Stake

  1. Fejezd Be KYC. Submit a érvényes government-issued photo ID in your Stake profile settings before the first kifizetés.
  2. Open the Kifizetés section of the cashier.
  3. Válaszd Ki Google Pay (UPI). Add Meg your GPay VPA. Standard formats: mobilnumber@okaxis, mobilnumber@okhdfcbank, mobilnumber@okicici, mobilnumber@oksbi.
  4. Add Meg the kifizetés amount. Double-check the VPA. An error in the VPA will cause the funds to reach the wrong fiókot with no recourse.
  5. Submit. The wallet deducts immediately. Funds arrive in 15 minutes to 3 hours; you will get a GPay notification on credit.

Limitek és határidők

Google Pay on UPI inherits the NPCI per-transaction limit of 1,00,000 INR. Google also sets its own napi aggregate limit, which for fully KYC-ellenőrzött fiókots is 1,00,000 INR per day. Some banks that participate in GPay set lower limits; check your bank's UPI limit in your net banking portal if you experience declines at high amounts.

For befizetések, timing is effectively azonnali: the NPCI switch erősítsd megs transactions in real time. For kifizetések, Stake's payment processor queues and dispatches the request, which typically fejezd bes within the first hour during the working day. Night-time kifizetések (midnight to 6 AM IST) may take slightly longer as the banking batch settlement runs.

No cap on the number of napi GPay befizetések from Stake's side, but if your bank declines after three failed attempts in a row, UPI may temporarily block further attempts to that VPA for up to 24 hours. In that case, use a different UPI app or crypto.

Ország availability and pénznem

Google Pay UPI is elérhető in India only. Stake processes GPay transactions exkluzívly in INR. If you have an overseas Google Pay fiókot, it operates on different payment rails and is not the same product; Stake's UPI integration applies only to Indian fiókots.

Google Pay is elérhető on Android and iOS. The app requires an Indian mobil number and an Indian bank fiókot to create a UPI VPA. As of 2024, Google Pay is the second-largest UPI app in India by transaction volume, ensuring high reliability and uptime.

Can I use Google Pay for Stake deposits in India?

Yes. Google Pay (GPay) uses the UPI rail and is fully supported at Stake for INR befizetések.

What Google Pay VPA format does Stake accept?

Stake accepts any érvényes UPI VPA including the standard Google Pay formats: mobilnumber@okaxis, mobilnumber@okhdfcbank, mobilnumber@okicici, mobilnumber@oksbi.

Is there a Google Pay withdrawal option at Stake?

Yes. Add Meg your GPay VPA in the kifizetés form. Funds land in the bank fiókot linked to that VPA within 15 minutes to 3 hours.

My GPay payment was declined. Why?

Common reasons: napi UPI limit reached on your bank fiókot, incorrect VPA, or your bank temporarily flagging the transaction. Try a smaller amount or switch to a different UPI app on the same fiókot.

No. Stake does not charge any fee. Google Pay itself does not charge for UPI P2P transfers in India.
