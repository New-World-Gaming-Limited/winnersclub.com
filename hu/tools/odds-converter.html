Hogyan működik ez a kalkulátor

All betting szorzók are just different ways of expressing the same underlying valószínűség. The szorzók converter takes any single format as input and derives a decimal representation first, then converts outward to every other format using the exact mathematical relationships between them.

Decimal szorzók represent total return per unit tétd, including your tét. An szorzók of 2.50 means a 1-unit bet returns 2.50 units total (1.50 nyereség). This is the native format at Stake and most European sportkönyvs.

Fractional szorzók are written as numerator/denominator (e.g. 6/4). Nyereség equals tét multiplied by numerator/denominator. A 6/4 bet on a 10-unit tét yields 15 units nyereség.

American (moneyline) szorzók use positive numbers for underdogs and negative numbers for favorites. +150 means a 100-unit tét wins 150 nyereség. -200 means you need to tét 200 to win 100 nyereség.

Hong Kong szorzók equal decimal szorzók minus 1 (just the nyereség per unit). A decimal of 2.50 equals Hong Kong 1.50.

Indonesian szorzók work like American but scaled per unit: positive values show nyereség per unit tétd; negative values show how much you tét per unit of nyereség.

Malay szorzók range from -1 to +1. Positive values represent nyereség per unit; negative values represent szükséges tét per unit of nyereség. A Malay of -0.67 approximates American -150.

The implied valószínűség formula is: valószínűség = 1 / decimal szorzók. A price of 2.50 carries 40% implied valószínűség. Comparing this figure against your own assessed valószínűség tells you whether a bet has positive expected value.

All conversions are lossless within floating-point precision. Fractional szorzók are approximated to the nearest standard fraction when displaying (e.g. 1.5/1 rounds to 3/2), though the calculator uses the exact decimal internally for all outputs.

Gyakori Kérdések

What is the most common odds format?

Decimal szorzók are the global standard used in Europe, Australia, and at Stake. Fractional szorzók remain népszerű in the UK. American (moneyline) szorzók dominate North American piacok.

How do I convert decimal to fractional odds?

Subtract 1 from the decimal, then express as a fraction in lowest terms. Decimal 2.50 becomes (2.50-1)/1 = 3/2 (written as 3/2 or "3 to 2").

What does implied probability mean?

Implied valószínűség is the percentage chance a bookmaker's price implies for an outcome. It equals 1 divided by the decimal szorzók. If the piac prices an outcome at 2.00, the implied valószínűség is 50%.

How are American odds converted to decimal?

For positive American szorzók: decimal = (American / 100) + 1. For negative: decimal = (100 / |American|) + 1. So +200 = 3.00 decimal and -200 = 1.50 decimal.

What is the overround and why does it matter?

The overround (also called vig or juice) is the sum of all implied probabilities in a piac minus 100%. A 5% overround means the book keeps roughly 5 cents per dollar wagered. Lower overround means better value for the bettor.
