Як deposit with a RuPay card at Stake

  1. Enable online card transactions. Before attempting a card deposit, open your bank's мобільний додаток and confirm online/e-commerce transactions are enabled for your RuPay card. Set the daily online limit to cover your intended deposit.
  2. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Депозит" and select Card or RuPay from the payment options.
  3. Enter your card details. Input the 16-digit card number, expiry date, and CVV. Confirm the amount (minimum 500 INR).
  4. Complete 3D Secure verification. Your bank will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP on the payment page within the time limit (usually 3 minutes).
  5. Wait for confirmation. The payment processes in under 5 minutes. Your Stake wallet updates and you receive an email confirmation.

Як withdraw when using RuPay at Stake

  1. Complete KYC first. Upload your Aadhaar or PAN card in your Stake profile.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Since direct card withdrawals to RuPay are limited, use UPI as the withdrawal method.
  3. Link a UPI VPA. Enter your UPI VPA (from any UPI app, including one linked to the same bank as your RuPay card).
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Confirm the VPA, then submit.
  5. Track the transfer. UPI withdrawal completes in 15 minutes to 3 hours. You will get an in-app notification from Stake and an SMS from your bank.

Ліміти та терміни

RuPay card deposit limits at Stake are controlled by your bank, not Stake. Standard daily online limits for Indian debit cards range from 25,000 INR to 1,00,000 INR depending on the bank and акаунт type. RuPay Classic cards typically have lower limits than RuPay Platinum cards.

If your bank blocks gaming merchant category codes (MCC 7995), the RuPay card deposit will be declined. In this case, UPI is a seamless alternative because UPI transfers use P2P rails that bypass MCC restrictions. Crypto deposits (Bitcoin, USDT) are also fully available as a fallback and have no MCC classification.

Депозит processing via RuPay card is instant to 5 minutes. There is no batch settlement like NEFT; the card payment network confirms in real time.

Доступність за країною та валюта

RuPay is India's domestic card scheme, launched by NPCI in 2012. It is accepted at all Indian point-of-sale terminals and most e-commerce merchants. Stake processes RuPay deposits in INR only. RuPay has expanded internationally to a few countries (UAE, Singapore, Bhutan), but Stake's RuPay acceptance applies to Indian-issued cards only.

RuPay cards are issued by most Indian banks including SBI, Bank of Baroda, Punjab National Bank, Canara Bank, and many cooperative banks. Jan Dhan Yojana акаунтs come with a free RuPay card, making this payment method accessible to a very large segment of the Indian population.

FAQ: RuPay at Stake

Can I use a RuPay debit card at Stake?

Так. RuPay debit cards are accepted at Stake for INR deposits. Ensure your card has online transactions enabled through your bank's app or net banking.

Does Stake accept RuPay credit cards?

RuPay credit card acceptance depends on the Stake payment gateway at the time of deposit. Debit cards are more reliably accepted. If a credit card is declined, use UPI or IMPS as an alternative.

What is the minimum RuPay card deposit at Stake?

Minimum card deposit is 500 INR.

My RuPay card was declined. Why?

Common reasons: online transactions not enabled, daily debit card limit reached, bank blocking gaming merchant category code (MCC 7995), or 3D Secure OTP failure. Enable online transactions in your bank app and try again.

Can I withdraw to a RuPay card from Stake?

Direct card refunds to RuPay are not standard; withdrawals typically go via UPI or bank transfer. Check the cashier for current withdrawal methods available on your акаунт.
