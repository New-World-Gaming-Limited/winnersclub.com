Як deposit with UPI at Stake

  1. Log in to your Stake account. If you do not have one yet, register and enter промокод NEWBONUS on the registration form.
  2. Open the Cashier. Click the green "Депозит" button in the top navigation bar. The cashier panel opens on the right side of the screen.
  3. Select UPI. In the payment method list, choose UPI. Enter the INR amount you want to deposit (minimum 300 INR).
  4. Complete the UPI payment. Stake's payment gateway redirects you to a UPI deep link or displays a VPA and QR code. Open your UPI app (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, or any other), scan the QR or enter the VPA, confirm the amount, and approve with your UPI PIN.
  5. Wait for confirmation. Your Stake wallet updates within 30 seconds. If the balance has not changed after 2 minutes, note the UTR shown in your UPI app and contact live-chat support.

Як withdraw with UPI from Stake

  1. Ensure KYC is complete. Stake requires identity verification before the first withdrawal. Upload a government-issued photo ID through the Profile section.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Click the wallet icon, then "Виведення."
  3. Choose UPI as the withdrawal method. Enter your registered UPI VPA (e.g., yourname@okhdfcbank or yourphone@ybl).
  4. Enter the amount. Specify the INR amount. Confirm the VPA is correct; withdrawals sent to a wrong VPA cannot be reversed.
  5. Submit and wait. Most UPI withdrawals process within 15 minutes to 3 hours. During peak hours or public holidays, bank settlement may extend to 24 hours. You will receive an in-app notification and email when the transfer completes.

Ліміти та терміни для UPI на Stake

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) caps individual UPI transactions at 1,00,000 INR (1 lakh). This is a hard ceiling applied at the NPCI infrastructure level, not a Stake policy. If you want to deposit more in a session, place multiple transactions with a short gap between them.

There is no published weekly cap on UPI deposits at Stake, but Stake's risk team may request additional identity checks if deposit patterns spike unusually. On the withdrawal side, Stake processes requests in the order received. The first withdrawal of a calendar month may take slightly longer (up to 4 hours) because the compliance team performs a routine акаунт огляд on first withdrawals.

Timing note: UPI payments are 24/7, including bank holidays and weekends, because NPCI operates around the clock. The 15 minute to 3 hour withdrawal window is the standard range; most complete in under an hour during business hours.

Доступність за країною та валюта

UPI is available exclusively to Indian residents holding a bank акаунт registered with a UPI-enabled institution. Stake processes UPI payments in INR only. Ні currency conversion occurs: you deposit in INR, your wallet balance shows in INR, and you withdraw in INR.

All major Indian banks are UPI-enabled: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Так Bank, and over 300 others. If your bank is UPI-enabled but the payment fails, the most common cause is a daily limit already reached on your UPI app or a wrong VPA entry. Double-check the VPA on the payment screen before confirming.

FAQ: UPI deposits and withdrawals at Stake

What is the minimum UPI deposit at Stake?

The minimum UPI deposit at Stake is 300 INR. There is no Stake fee on the transfer; your bank or UPI app may round up to the nearest rupee.

How long does a UPI deposit take to reach my Stake account?

UPI deposits are processed instantly. Your balance should update within 30 seconds of completing payment in your UPI app.

Can I withdraw to UPI from Stake?

Так. UPI withdrawals are supported. Processing typically completes within 15 minutes to 3 hours, depending on banking infrastructure load.

My UPI deposit shows pending. What should I do?

Wait 10 minutes. If funds left your bank but did not credit your Stake wallet, open live-chat support and share your UTR (UPI Transaction Reference) number. The team can trace and post the deposit manually.

Are there daily UPI deposit limits?

RBI mandates a daily UPI transaction cap of 1 lakh INR (100,000 INR) per VPA. Stake's own per-transaction ceiling aligns with this. If you need to deposit more in a single day, split across multiple transactions or use crypto as an alternative.
