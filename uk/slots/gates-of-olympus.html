How Gates of Olympus works

Gates of Olympus is a 6x5 grid slot using Pragmatic Play's Ante-bet tumble system. Like Sweet Bonanza, it uses a pay-anywhere mechanic: 8 or more identical symbols anywhere on the reels form a win. There are no fixed paylines. After any win, matching symbols explode and new ones fall from above; this continues until no new win forms.

The low-pay symbols are card suits rendered in gemstone style. The premium symbols are Zeus, Poseidon, Hades, and other Greek god imagery. The Zeus symbol is the highest-paying: 12+ Zeus symbols covering a substantial portion of the grid can produce massive returns before multiplier application.

The multiplier symbol is Zeus himself, depicted holding a lightning bolt. This random multiplier (2x through 500x) lands anywhere on the reels and applies during tumbles in the base game as well as during безкоштовні оберти. Multiple multiplier symbols on one spin multiply together.

Scatter symbols are golden hourglasses. Landing 4, 5, or 6 scatters in one spin triggers the безкоштовні оберти feature. The 6x5 layout and the multiplier mechanic operating in the base game distinguishes this from many comparable слоти. Base game multipliers mean meaningful wins are achievable without the безкоштовні оберти бонус, though very high volatility means these events are infrequent.

Бонус features

Four scatters pay out the ante value and trigger 15 безкоштовні оберти. Five scatters award 15 spins; six award 15 as well, but with increased multiplier frequencies. During безкоштовні оберти, every Zeus multiplier symbol that lands adds its value to a persistent global multiplier. Unlike the base game where multipliers apply per tumble, the безкоштовні оберти feature accumulates them on a meter that resets at the start of the feature.

Landing additional scatters during безкоштовні оберти extends the session: 3 scatters retrigger another 15 spins. The persistent multiplier accumulation mechanic means that a particularly lucky безкоштовні оберти session with many multiplier symbols can stack an enormous global multiplier before landing a high-coverage win on premium symbols.
