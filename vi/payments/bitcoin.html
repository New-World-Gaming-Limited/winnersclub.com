Hạn mức và thời gian xử lý for Bitcoin nạp tiềns and rút tiềns

Bitcoin confirmations take on average 10 minutes per block on the Bitcoin network. Stake requires 3 confirmations, so expect 20 to 40 minutes for a standard nạp tiền to credit. During periods of network congestion, blocks may take longer; track your transaction on mempool.space using your TXID.

The Bitcoin network fee (miner fee) is set by market demand for block space. In low-congestion periods, fees can be as low as a few satoshis per byte (under $1 USD total). During high congestion (bull markets or after halving events), fees can spike to $5 to $50+ per transaction. Use a wallet that allows you to set a custom fee: higher fee = nhanher confirmation.

There is no Stake-imposed tối đa on Bitcoin nạp tiềns. The practical limits are the size of your holdings and the Bitcoin network's own processing capacity.
