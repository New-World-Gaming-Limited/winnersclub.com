IMPS nạp tiền at Stake for Indian players

IMPS (Immediate Thanh toán Service) lets Indian players send INR directly from their bank account to Stake without any app or wallet intermediary. Stake is licensed under Curaçao 1668/JAZ and serves 400,000+ players. IMPS supports up to 5 lakh INR per transaction, making it the preferred choice for large nạp tiềns where UPI's 1 lakh cap is restrictive. How to nạp tiền using IMPS at Stake

  1. Log in and open the Cashier. Click "Nạp tiền" and select IMPS from the thanh toán list.
  2. Note the Stake bank details. The cashier displays a beneficiary account number, IFSC code, and account name. Write these down or screenshot them.
  3. Open your bank's net banking or ứng dụng di động. Navigate to "Fund Transfer" or "IMPS Transfer."
  4. Enter the beneficiary details. Input the account number and IFSC code from the Stake cashier. Enter the nạp tiền amount (tối thiểu 500 INR). Add your Stake user ID in the thanh toán remarks/reference field so the thanh toán team can match your transfer.
  5. Submit the transfer and wait. Your bank will send an SMS with a UTR number. Keep this. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 to 30 minutes. If not updated in 30 minutes, share the UTR with live-chat support.

How to withdraw via IMPS from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Provide a government-issued ID and proof of bank account (cancelled cheque or bank statement) in your Stake profile.
  2. Open Cashier and select Rút tiền. Choose IMPS.
  3. Enter your bank account details. Provide your full account number, IFSC code, and account holder name exactly as registered with your bank.
  4. Enter the rút tiền amount. Tối thiểu varies; check the cashier screen.
  5. Submit. Stake initiates the IMPS transfer within 1 to 2 hours. End-to-end completion is 1 to 6 hours. Your bank will credit the amount and send an SMS confirmation.

Hạn mức và thời gian xử lý

IMPS has a per-transaction cap of 5,00,000 INR (5 lakh) set by NPCI, which is significantly higher than UPI's 1 lakh limit. This makes IMPS the best option for high-value nạp tiềns. Individual banks may impose lower per-transaction limits; check your net banking settings or contact your bank to confirm your IMPS limit.

IMPS operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including public holidays and weekend banking non-business days. However, very large transfers (above 2 lakh) may trigger an additional review delay on the bank's fraud prevention system, adding 15 to 30 minutes to the posting time.

Các ngân hàng thường tính 5 đến 25 INR mỗi giao dịch IMPS. Phí này do ngân hàng của bạn khấu trừ; Stake không tính thêm phí xử lý nào.

Quốc gia hỗ trợ và tiền tệ

IMPS is có sẵn across all NPCI member banks in India, covering virtually every bank in the country. Transactions are in INR only. IMPS is governed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) under the Thanh toán and Settlement Systems Act, 2007.

Players who prefer not to use a third-party app (like PhonePe or GPay) can use IMPS directly from their bank's net banking portal or ứng dụng di động, giving more control over the transfer details.

Alternative thanh toán methods

  • UPI - app-based ngay lập tức transfer, 1 lakh cap
  • NEFT - lower fee, slightly slower, no transaction cap
  • Net banking - covers both NEFT and IMPS in one flow
  • Bitcoin - tiền điện tử alternative for large nạp tiềns without bank limits
  • USDT ERC20 - stablecoin for large value transfers

Câu hỏi thường gặp: IMPS at Stake

What is IMPS and how is it different from UPI at Stake?

IMPS (Immediate Thanh toán Service) is a direct interbank transfer using IFSC code and account number, while UPI uses a virtual thanh toán address. IMPS allows transfers up to 5 lakh INR per transaction compared to UPI's 1 lakh cap.

How long does an IMPS nạp tiền take to reach Stake?

IMPS is ngay lập tức, 24/7. Funds typically appear in your Stake wallet within 5 to 30 minutes after your bank confirms the transfer.

What details do I need to make an IMPS nạp tiền at Stake?

You need the Stake beneficiary account number and IFSC code, which are displayed in the cashier when you select IMPS.

Is there a tối thiểu amount for IMPS nạp tiềns at Stake?

Yes, tối thiểu is 500 INR for IMPS nạp tiềns at Stake.

My IMPS transfer was successful but Stake balance did not update. What do I do?

IMPS reconciliation can take up to 30 minutes. If balance is still not updated after 30 minutes, provide your bank UTR number and the RRN (Reference Number) to Stake live-chat for manual credit.
