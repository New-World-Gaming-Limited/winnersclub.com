How to nạp tiền with Boleto at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Nạp tiền" and select Boleto from the thanh toán method list.
  2. Enter the nạp tiền amount. Input the BRL amount (tối thiểu R$30). A boleto PDF or barcode is generated on screen.
  3. Save or print the boleto. The boleto has a barcode and a numeric string (linha digitavel). Note the due date; boletos typically expire within 1 to 3 days.
  4. Pay the boleto. You can pay via your bank's app (scan the barcode), Loteria (lottery kiosk), ATM, or any bank branch. Thanh toán via banking app is nhanhest. Enter the barcode number or scan the QR code if your bank supports boleto QR.
  5. Wait for processing. After thanh toán, boleto takes 1 to 3 business days to clear. Your Stake balance updates once the bank confirms settlement. If you need funds credited nhanher for an upcoming event, use PIX instead.

How to withdraw from Stake as a Brazilian player (not via Boleto)

  1. Boleto is nạp tiền-only. For rút tiềns, use PIX via the cashier rút tiền section.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Rút tiền.
  3. Choose PIX. Enter your CPF-linked PIX key or phone number.
  4. Enter the rút tiền amount. Confirm the PIX key details match your registered name.
  5. Submit. PIX rút tiềns process within 30 to 60 minutes from Stake's side; the actual bank transfer completes in seconds.

Hạn mức và thời gian xử lý

Boleto processing is not ngay lập tức. After you pay, the clearing house (CIP or Febraban) processes the thanh toán, which takes 1 to 2 business days at tối thiểu. Weekend thanh toáns (Saturday or Sunday) typically do not settle until Monday or Tuesday. Public holidays add one business day each.

For time-sensitive nạp tiềns, PIX is the clear superior option. Boleto is best used when you plan ahead and want to fund your Stake account days before an event or tournament.

The boleto face value must exactly match the amount entered in the Stake cashier. If you pay a different amount (e.g., rounding up), the entire thanh toán may be rejected and returned to you after 3 to 5 business days.

Tối đa boleto values vary by the issuing bank. Most Stake boletos cap at R$10,000 BRL per slip. For larger nạp tiềns, generate multiple boletos or use PIX or tiền điện tử.

Quốc gia hỗ trợ và tiền tệ

Boleto is có sẵn in Brazil only. It is a Brazilian-specific thanh toán instrument regulated by Febraban and Banco Central do Brasil. All boleto transactions at Stake are in BRL. Players outside Brazil cannot use boleto.

Boleto is accepted at all Brazilian banks and 450,000+ thanh toán kiosks (including Casas Loterica, Correios post offices, and supermarkets). It remains one of the most used thanh toán instruments in Brazil, particularly for players who prefer not to use a bank app or do not have PIX set up.

Câu hỏi thường gặp: Boleto at Stake

How long does a Boleto nạp tiền take to process at Stake?

Boleto nạp tiềns typically take 1 to 3 business days to confirm. Unlike PIX, boleto requires bank batch processing. If you need an ngay lập tức nạp tiền, use PIX instead.

What is the tối thiểu Boleto nạp tiền at Stake?

Tối thiểu Boleto nạp tiền is R$30 BRL.

Can I withdraw from Stake using Boleto?

No. Boleto is a nạp tiền-only method. Rút tiềns at Stake for Brazilian players are done via PIX, which sends directly to your bank account.

What happens if I let my Boleto expire before paying?

An expired boleto cannot be paid. You will need to request a new one from the Stake cashier. Boleto expiry is typically 1 to 3 days from issuance.

Does Stake charge any fee for Boleto nạp tiềns?

Stake does not charge a fee. Some banks charge up to R$3 for processing a boleto thanh toán; this is a bank fee, not a Stake charge.
