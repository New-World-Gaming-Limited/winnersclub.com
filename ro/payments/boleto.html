How to deposit with Boleto at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Deposit" and select Boleto from the payment method list.
  2. Enter the deposit amount. Input the BRL amount (minimum R$30). A boleto PDF or barcode is generated on screen.
  3. Save or print the boleto. The boleto has a barcode and a numeric string (linha digitavel). Note the due date; boletos typically expire within 1 to 3 days.
  4. Pay the boleto. You can pay via your bank's app (scan the barcode), Loteria (lottery kiosk), ATM, or any bank branch. Payment via banking app is fastest. Enter the barcode number or scan the QR code if your bank supports boleto QR.
  5. Wait for processing. After payment, boleto takes 1 to 3 business days to clear. Your Stake balance updates once the bank confirms settlement. If you need funds credited faster for an upcoming event, use PIX instead.

How to withdraw from Stake as a Brazilian player (not via Boleto)

  1. Boleto is deposit-only. For retrageres, use PIX via the cashier retragere section.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retragere.
  3. Choose PIX. Enter your CPF-linked PIX key or phone number.
  4. Enter the retragere amount. Confirm the PIX key details match your registered name.
  5. Submit. PIX retrageres process within 30 to 60 minutes from Stake's side; the actual bank transfer completes in seconds.

Limite și termene

Boleto processing is not instant. After you pay, the clearing house (CIP or Febraban) processes the payment, which takes 1 to 2 business days at minimum. Weekend payments (Saturday or Sunday) typically do not settle until Monday or Tuesday. Public holidays add one business day each.

For time-sensitive deposits, PIX is the clear superior option. Boleto is best used when you plan ahead and want to fund your Stake account days before an event or tournament.

The boleto face value must exactly match the amount entered in the Stake cashier. If you pay a different amount (e.g., rounding up), the entire payment may be rejected and returned to you after 3 to 5 business days.

Maximum boleto values vary by the issuing bank. Most Stake boletos cap at R$10,000 BRL per slip. For larger deposits, generate multiple boletos or use PIX or crypto.

FAQ: Boleto at Stake

How long does a Boleto deposit take to process at Stake?

Boleto deposits typically take 1 to 3 business days to confirm. Unlike PIX, boleto requires bank batch processing. If you need an instant deposit, use PIX instead.

What is the minimum Boleto deposit at Stake?

Minimum Boleto deposit is R$30 BRL.

Can I withdraw from Stake using Boleto?

No. Boleto is a deposit-only method. Retrageres at Stake for Brazilian players are done via PIX, which sends directly to your bank account.

What happens if I let my Boleto expire before paying?

An expired boleto cannot be paid. You will need to request a new one from the Stake cashier. Boleto expiry is typically 1 to 3 days from issuance.

Does Stake charge any fee for Boleto deposits?

Stake does not charge a fee. Some banks charge up to R$3 for processing a boleto payment; this is a bank fee, not a Stake charge.
