How to deposit via net banking at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Deposit" and select Net Banking from the payment options.
  2. Select your bank. A list of supported banks appears. Choose your bank from the dropdown. If your bank is not listed, use UPI or IMPS as alternatives.
  3. Enter the deposit amount. Input the INR amount (minimum 500 INR). Confirm and click "Proceed to Bank."
  4. Log in on your bank's secure page. You are redirected to your bank's own net banking login. Enter your user ID and password. Complete any additional verification (OTP, grid card, etc.) required by your bank.
  5. Authorise the payment. Confirm the transfer on your bank's portal. You are redirected back to Stake. The wallet updates within 5 to 120 minutes depending on whether the transfer routes via IMPS or NEFT.

How to withdraw to your bank account from Stake

  1. Complete KYC verification. Submit identity documents in your Stake profile before the first retragere.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retragere. Choose Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS).
  3. Enter your bank account details. Provide the account number, IFSC code, account holder name, and bank name.
  4. Enter the retragere amount. Confirm all details on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake debits your wallet immediately and initiates the bank transfer. Funds arrive in 1 to 12 hours via NEFT or 30 minutes to 3 hours via IMPS. Your bank sends an SMS on credit.

Limite și termene

Net banking deposit limits at Stake are constrained by your bank's daily online transfer limit, which varies. SBI allows up to 10 lakh INR per day via net banking; HDFC and ICICI allow up to 25 lakh per day for retail customers. Check your bank's limit in your net banking profile under "Transaction Limits."

Stake's payment aggregator routes the transaction via IMPS for faster settlement or NEFT for larger amounts, depending on the aggregator's routing logic. In practice, most standard deposits settle within 30 minutes during business hours.

Net banking requires an active internet connection and access to your bank's portal. If your bank enforces session time limits, complete the Stake payment page quickly. Sessions that expire mid-flow may result in a pending transaction that settles automatically within 30 minutes or returns to your bank account.
