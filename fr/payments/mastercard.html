Comment déposer with Mastercard at Stake

  1. Enable online/international transactions. Open your bank app and confirm your Mastercard has online e-commerce and international transactions enabled. Set an appropriate quotidien limit.
  2. Se connecter to Stake and open the Caisse. S'inscrire if new; enter code promo MAXBET.
  3. Select Mastercard (or Card) from options de paiement. Enter the dépôt amount.
  4. Enter card details. 16-digit card number, expiry date, CVV (3 digits on back), and cardholder name as printed.
  5. Complete 3D Secure (Mastercard Identity Check). An OTP or push notification from your bank arrives. Applicationrove within the time limit. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 minutes.

Comment retirer to Mastercard from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Submit photo ID and card ownership proof before the first retrait.
  2. Open the Caisse and select Retrait. Check whether card retrait is listed.
  3. If available, select Mastercard. Enter card number and retrait amount. Submit.
  4. Await the refund. Card retraits take 3 to 5 business days to appear due to Mastercard's refund délai de traitementline. This is not a Stake delay.
  5. If card retrait is not listed, use a bank transfer or portefeuille électronique option in the caisse instead.

Limites et délais

Mastercard dépôt limits at Stake are determined by your issuing bank. Most banks set quotidien online purchase limits between $1,000 and $5,000 USD for standard cards; premium and infinite cards may have higher limits. Check your bank app under "Card Limits" or call your bank to confirm.

For Inden players: Inden banks often apply gaming MCC (7995) blocks to both Visa and Mastercard. If your Mastercard is declined, UPI is the most reliable alternative for INR dépôts. Crypto (Bitcoin, USDT TRC20) works without any MCC classification and est disponible regardless of which bank issued your card.

3D Secure (Mastercard Identity Check) is required for all Mastercard transactions at Stake. This is a security layer that protects your card from unauthorized use. Ensure your bank has your current phone number for OTP delivery.

FAQ: Mastercard at Stake

Does Stake accept Mastercard debit and credit cards?

Oui. Stake accepts Mastercard debit, credit, and prepaid cards. Online transactions must be enabled on your card before dépôting.

What is the minimum Mastercard dépôt at Stake?

Minimum Mastercard dépôt at Stake is $10 USD or monnaie locale equivalent.

My Mastercard was declined at Stake. Why?

Common reasons: gaming MCC block by your bank, exceeded quotidien limit, failed 3D Secure OTP, or international transaction restriction. Enable international transactions in your banking app and retry. If declined again, use crypto or an portefeuille électronique.

Can I withdraw from Stake to my Mastercard?

Card retraits to Mastercard may be available; check the caisse. If not, use bank transfer or an portefeuille électronique for retraits.

Does Stake charge extra for Mastercard dépôts?

Stake does not charge any fee for Mastercard dépôts. Your bank may charge a foreign frais de transaction if the dépôt currency differs from your card currency.
