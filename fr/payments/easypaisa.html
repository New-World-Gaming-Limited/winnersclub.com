Comment déposer with Easypaisa at Stake

  1. Se connecter to Stake and open the Caisse. Nouveau users: register and enter code promo MAXBET.
  2. Select Easypaisa from the options de paiement. Enter the PKR amount (minimum PKR 500).
  3. Nonte the Stake Easypaisa account number displayed. This is the recipient account number you send money to.
  4. Open the Easypaisa app. Tap "Send Money," enter the Stake account number, confirm the amount. Enter your Easypaisa MPIN to authorise. Include your Stake user ID in the remarks/reference field.
  5. Wait for credit. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 minutes. Keep the Easypaisa transaction ID in case you need to contact support.

Comment retirer from Stake to Easypaisa

  1. Complete identity verification. Submit your CNIC (Computerised National Identity Card) details in your Stake profile before the first retrait.

  4. Enter the retrait amount. Confirm all details. Check that the name on your Easypaisa account matches your Stake registration.
  5. Submit. Stake queues the retrait. Funds arrive in your Easypaisa mobile account within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS from Easypaisa confirms receipt.

FAQ: Easypaisa at Stake

What is the minimum Easypaisa dépôt at Stake?

Minimum Easypaisa dépôt at Stake is PKR 500.

How long does an Easypaisa dépôt take?

Easypaisa dépôts are processed instantanély or within 5 minutes. Your Stake wallet reflects the balance as soon as the transaction is confirmed on Easypaisa's network.

Can I withdraw from Stake to my Easypaisa account?

Oui. Retraits to Easypaisa mobile accounts are supported. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours.

I do not have a mobile account. Can I use Easypaisa OTC?

Easypaisa OTC (over-the-counter at a franchise) requires a registered mobile account to receive the funds at Stake. A full Easypaisa mobile account is needed for Stake dépôts and retraits.

Does Stake charge a fee for Easypaisa transactions?

Stake charges no fee. Easypaisa may charge a standard wallet-to-wallet transfer fee; check the Easypaisa fee schedule in their app.
