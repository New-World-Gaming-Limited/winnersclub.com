Comment déposer Solana at Stake

  1. Se connecter to Stake and open the Caisse. Nouveau users: register and enter code promo NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Solana (SOL) from the cryptomonnaie dépôt options. Ensure you select the correct network: Solana. Sending SOL on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake SOL dépôt address. A unique adresse de portefeuille is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send SOL from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake dépôt address, enter the amount (minimum 0.1 SOL), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmations. The Solana network requires 20 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. Under 30 seconds (20 Solana confirmations).

Comment retirer Solana from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto retraits.
  2. Open the Caisse and select Retrait. Choose Solana (SOL).
  3. Enter your SOL adresse de portefeuille. This must be an address on the Solana network. Double-check the address character by character; crypto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Enter the retrait amount. Stake deducts a frais de réseau from the amount to cover on-chain transaction costs. The fee is shown before you confirm.
  5. Submit and track the transaction. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the Solana network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Retrait processing takes 15 to 30 minutes after submission.

How long does a Solana dépôt take to confirm?

Under 30 seconds (20 Solana confirmations). Solana dépôts require 20 network confirmations before crediting to your Stake wallet.

Does Stake charge a fee for Solana dépôts or retraits?

Stake does not charge a dépôt fee. For retraits, Stake deducts a frais de réseau from the retrait amount to cover the blockchain transaction cost. Solana frais de réseaus are a fraction of a cent per transaction, among the lowest in crypto.
