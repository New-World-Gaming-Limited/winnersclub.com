FAQ: Stake méthodes de paiement

Which méthode de paiement is fastest for dépôts at Stake?

For crypto: Solana (SOL) and Tron (TRX) confirm in under 30 seconds. USDT TRC20 confirms in under 2 minutes. For fiat: UPI (Inde), PIX (Brésil), M-Pesa (Africa), and MuchBetter (mondial) all credit in under 60 seconds. Standard bank transfers like NEFT and Boleto take longer due to batch settlement.

Does Stake charge any fees on dépôts or retraits?

Stake charges zero fees on dépôts and retraits for all 32 méthodes de paiement. Crypto retraits include a frais de réseau that is deducted from the sent amount; this is a blockchain fee, not a Stake charge. Provider fees (bank charges, mobile money fees, card foreign frais de transactions) are set by the payment provider and are outside Stake's control.

Which méthode de paiement is best for Inden players at Stake?

UPI is the fastest and most convenient for Inden players. IMPS is best for large single transfers (jusqu'à 5 lakh INR). NEFT has no transaction cap. If UPI/bank methods are declining, USDT TRC20 or Bitcoin are geographic-agnostic alternatives with no RBI restrictions.

How do I withdraw winnings from Stake?

Open the Caisse, click Retrait, and choose your preferred method. Complete KYC (identity verification) in your Profile before the first retrait. Most portefeuille électronique and mobile money retraits complete in 15 minutes to 3 hours. Bank transfers take 1 to 5 business days. Crypto retraits take 10 to 60 minutes deen attente on the network.

Is my payment informations safe at Stake?

Oui. Stake uses PCI DSS-compliant payment gateways. Card details are processed by the payment provider, not stored on Stake's servers. Crypto dépôts use a unique adresse de portefeuille per user session. Portefeuille électronique dépôts do not expose bank details to Stake.
