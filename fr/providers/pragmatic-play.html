Game mechanics Pragmatic Play is known for

The Tumble feature (Cascading Rouleaux) is present in most of the studio's high-volatilité titles. When a winning combination lands, those symbols are removed and new ones fall from above -- repeating until no winning combination appears. This creates multi-win chains from a single bet, which is why titles like Gates of Olympus can produce four-figure multiplicateurs from base bets during a single tours gratuits round.

Multiplicateur bombs are a related mechanic appearing in Sweet Bonanza, Olympus, et autres. During tours gratuits, random symbole multiplicateurs land on the grid and their values sum together (rather than multiply with each other). A round that lands 2x + 5x + 10x will apply a 17x multiplicateur to that tumble's win -- an additive model that keeps peak payouts in the 5,000x-21,100x range.

The Buy Feature (Ante Bet) option appears on most titles and allows players to pay 100x their bet to directly purchase a tours gratuits round. This is disabled in the UK marché due to UKGC regulations but active at Stake under Curaçao 1668/JAZ rules.
