Jak deposit with PhonePe at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake. If you do not have an konto, register and enter MAXBET to activate the bonus powitalny.
  2. Open the Cashier. Tap the "Wpłata" button in the top navigation. The payment panel slides open.
  3. Select PhonePe. From the list of payment options, choose PhonePe. Input the INR amount (minimum 300 INR).
  4. Complete the PhonePe payment. You will be shown a QR code or a UPI deep link. If deep link, it will open the PhonePe app directly. Approve the payment using your PhonePe UPI PIN. If using QR, open PhonePe, tap "Scan QR," and scan the displayed code.
  5. Confirm the balance update. Return to Stake. Your wallet should reflect the deposit in under 60 seconds. Niete the PhonePe transaction ID from your app in case you need to raise a query.

Jak withdraw via PhonePe from Stake

  1. Verify your identity. Complete KYC (government ID upload) in your Stake Profile before your first withdrawal.
  2. Go to the Cashier and click Withdraw.
  3. Select PhonePe (UPI). Enter your PhonePe VPA (example: 9XXXXXXXXX@ybl or yourname@axisbank).
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Check the minimum withdrawal requirement shown on screen. Confirm the VPA is correct.
  5. Submit the request. The amount debits from your Stake wallet immediately. Funds arrive in your bank konto linked to PhonePe within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS from PhonePe confirms credit.

Limity i czasy realizacji

PhonePe transactions run on NPCI's UPI network, which enforces a 1,00,000 INR per-transaction ceiling. There is also a per-day cap across all UPI apps on a single bank konto, typically 1,00,000 INR aggregated. If you approach this limit, you will need to wait until midnight for the counter to reset, or use a secondary UPI-linked konto.

Własny portfel PhonePe (odrębny od UPI) narzuca niższe limity 10 000 INR na transakcję i 20 000 INR miesięcznie dla portfeli bez KYC. Stake akceptuje przepływ UPI VPA, a nie saldo portfela PhonePe, więc obowiązują wyższe limity NPCI.

Wypłataals at Stake follow a standard queue. First-time withdrawals within a 30-day window may take up to 4 hours due to compliance checks. Subsequent withdrawals in the same month typically process within 30 to 90 minutes.

FAQ: PhonePe at Stake

Does Stake support direct PhonePe deposits?

Tak. PhonePe is available in the Stake cashier as a dedicated option. It uses the UPI rail, so the flow is the same as any UPI payment but pre-selects the PhonePe app.

What is the minimum PhonePe deposit at Stake?

Minimum is 300 INR, consistent with UPI limits set by NPCI.

Can I withdraw to my PhonePe wallet from Stake?

Wypłataals go to the bank konto linked to your PhonePe VPA. Direct wallet credit is not supported; the amount lands in your savings konto.

PhonePe shows payment failed but money was deducted. What now?

Do not retry immediately. Open Stake live-chat, provide your PhonePe transaction ID (found in PhonePe transaction history). Most auto-reversals complete within 5 minutes; manual credit takes up to 2 hours.

Is PhonePe available 24/7 for Stake deposits?

Tak. PhonePe operates 24/7 on the UPI network. Even on bank holidays, the NPCI switch routes the transaction to your bank's UPI service.
