Jak deposit Tron at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter kod promocyjny NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Tron (TRX) from the cryptocurrency deposit options. Ensure you select the correct network: Tron. Sending TRX on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake TRX deposit address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send TRX from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake deposit address, enter the amount (minimum 50 TRX), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmations. The Tron network requires 20 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. Under 2 minutes (20 Tron confirmations at 3-second blocks).

Jak withdraw Tron from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto withdrawals.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Tron (TRX).
  3. Enter your TRX wallet address. This must be an address on the Tron network. Double-check the address character by character; crypto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Stake deducts a network fee from the amount to cover on-chain transaction costs. The fee is shown before you confirm.
  5. Submit and track the transaction. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the Tron network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Wypłataal processing takes 10 to 30 minutes after submission.

Limity i czasy realizacji for Tron deposits and withdrawals

Tron is one of the fastest major blockchains. Block time is approximately 3 seconds, and Stake requires 20 confirmations, meaning deposits confirm in under 2 minutes. This is among the fastest deposit confirmation times of any cryptocurrency accepted at Stake.

Tron transaction fees are paid using bandwidth and energy resources. Standard TRX transfers cost a small amount of bandwidth, which is replenished over time for addresses with TRX staked. If you have sufficient bandwidth, TRX transfers can be effectively fee-free. For kontos without staked TRX, a small TRX amount is burned as the network fee, typically under 1 TRX ($0.10 USD or less).

Minimum TRX deposit at Stake is 50 TRX. There is no maximum from Stake's side. Tron's high throughput (2,000 transactions per second capacity) means congestion is rare.
