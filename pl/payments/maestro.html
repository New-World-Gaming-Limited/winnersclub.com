Jak deposit with Maestro at Stake

  1. Check your card. Confirm your card shows the Maestro logo on the front or back. Niete whether your Maestro card has a CVV (some older Maestro cards do not).
  2. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter kod promocyjny MAXBET.
  3. Select Maestro or Mastercard/Debit Card. Some gateways list Maestro separately; others route it through Mastercard.
  4. Enter your card details. Card number (typically 13 to 19 digits on Maestro), expiry date, CVV if your card has one, and cardholder name.
  5. Complete 3D Secure. Maestro cards use Mastercard Identity Check (3DS2). Approve the OTP or push notification from your bank. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 minutes upon approval.

Jak withdraw if you deposited via Maestro at Stake

  1. Complete KYC verification on Stake. Submit a photo ID before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. If Maestro/card withdrawal is not available, use bank transfer (NEFT, IMPS for Indie; SEPA for Europe) or an e-wallet.
  3. For bank transfer: Enter your konto number, IFSC/IBAN, and konto holder name matching your bank records.
  4. Enter withdrawal amount and submit.
  5. Await settlement. Bank transfers take 1 to 5 business days depending on the destination country and bank processing speed.

Limity i czasy realizacji

Maestro is a debit card, which means transactions debit directly from your bank konto. Daily debit limits are set by your bank, typically in the range of EUR 1,000 to EUR 5,000 per day for online transactions. Some banks have lower online Maestro limits than general debit limits; check with your bank.

Maestro card issuance was discontinued for new cards in many European markets from 2023 as Mastercard transitioned to Debit Mastercard. However, existing Maestro cards remain valid until their expiry date. If your Maestro card is nearing expiry, contact your bank for a replacement Debit Mastercard.

For Indien players holding Maestro-branded cards: Indien bank MCC blocks apply to Maestro cards the same way as Visa and Mastercard. If declined for gaming reasons, use UPI, IMPS, or crypto instead.

Dostępność według kraju i waluty

Maestro cards are primarily issued in Europe (Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, and eastern European countries) and a few other markets. UK banks have largely replaced Maestro with Debit Mastercard. Indien banks issue Maestro-branded RuPay cards but these operate on RuPay's domestic network. Stake accepts Maestro where supported by its payment gateway; if not listed in the cashier, use Mastercard or Visa.

FAQ: Maestro card at Stake

Is Maestro still accepted at Stake in 2024?

Maestro card acceptance depends on the payment processor. As Mastercard has phased out new Maestro issuance in some markets, check the Stake cashier for current Maestro availability. If not listed separately, your Maestro card may work under the general Mastercard option.

What is the minimum Maestro deposit at Stake?

Minimum Maestro deposit at Stake is $10 USD or local currency equivalent.

Can I withdraw from Stake to a Maestro card?

Maestro is typically debit-only and may not support card-back refunds depending on your bank. If card withdrawal fails, use bank transfer or e-wallet as the withdrawal method.

My Maestro card has no CVV. Can I still deposit at Stake?

Some Maestro cards do not have a CVV. In this case, the deposit form may accept '000' or leave CVV blank, depending on the gateway. Contact Stake live-chat for guidance if the form requires a CVV your card does not have.

Maestro was declined. What alternatives do I have?

Use a Visa or Mastercard credit/debit card, an e-wallet (Skrill, Neteller, EcoPayz), or crypto (Bitcoin, USDT TRC20) as alternatives at Stake.
