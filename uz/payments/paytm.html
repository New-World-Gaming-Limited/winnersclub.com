How to deposit with Paytm at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake. New users: register with Promokod MAXBET to activate the Xush kelibsiz bonus.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Deposit. The payment method panel loads.
  3. Choose Paytm or UPI. Some versions of the Stake cashier show Paytm as a dedicated tile; others show a generic UPI input. Either will work with a Paytm VPA.
  4. Enter amount and complete payment. Enter 300 INR or more. A QR code or deep-link appears. In the Paytm app, tap "Scan and Pay" or follow the deep-link, verify the amount, and approve with your Paytm UPI PIN.
  5. Confirm the credit. The Stake wallet updates within 60 seconds. Save the Paytm Order ID from the app in case you need to follow up.

How to withdraw to Paytm from Stake

  1. Complete identity verification. Upload an Aadhaar or PAN card scan in your Stake profile before requesting a withdrawal.
  2. Go to Cashier and select Withdrawal.
  3. Choose Paytm (UPI). Enter your Paytm VPA, typically mobilenumber@paytm.
  4. Specify the withdrawal amount. Verify the VPA on screen. Even a single digit error routes funds to the wrong account.
  5. Submit. Funds deduct from your wallet instantly and arrive in your Paytm-linked bank account within 15 minutes to 3 hours. A Paytm notification confirms receipt.

Limitlar va muddatlar

Paytm UPI transactions are subject to the same NPCI ceiling of 1,00,000 INR per transaction. Paytm's own daily transaction limit for fully verified accounts is also 1,00,000 INR. For the Paytm wallet (as opposed to UPI), limits are lower: 10,000 INR per transaction and 1,00,000 INR per month. Because Stake routes through UPI VPA, not the wallet balance, the higher limit applies.

During peak times like IPL season or major cricket matches, Paytm payment server load increases. If the payment takes more than 90 seconds to confirm, it is still being processed; do not restart the flow or you risk a duplicate transaction. Wait at least 3 minutes before concluding a payment has failed.

If your Paytm account is under Minimum KYC status, UPI limits may be lower than full KYC accounts. Upgrade to full KYC in the Paytm app for maximum limits.

FAQ: Paytm at Stake

Can I use Paytm Wallet for Stake deposits?

Stake accepts Paytm via the UPI VPA route. Deposits from the Paytm Wallet balance may not be available; use the Paytm UPI handle (mobilenumber@paytm) linked to your bank account instead.

What is the Paytm VPA format for Stake?

The standard Paytm UPI handle is mobilenumber@paytm. This VPA draws from the bank account linked in the Paytm app.

How long do Paytm deposits take at Stake?

Paytm UPI deposits are instant. Your Stake balance updates within 60 seconds of payment approval in the Paytm app.

Can I withdraw from Stake to Paytm?

Yes, withdrawals to the Paytm UPI VPA (linked bank account) are supported. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours.

Paytm deposit failed. What should I check?

First, confirm the Paytm UPI PIN was entered correctly. Second, check your linked bank account balance. Third, check if your bank has blocked gaming MCC transactions. If all clear, provide the Paytm Order ID to Stake live-chat.
