Jak deposit with Neteller at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Zaregistrovat se first if new; enter promo kód MAXBET.
  2. Select Neteller from the payment options. Enter the deposit amount in USD or EUR.
  3. Log in to Neteller. You are redirected to a Neteller-hosted page. Enter your Neteller účet email and secure ID.
  4. Approve the payment. Confirm the merchant name (Stake) and amount. Complete any 2FA required by Neteller (email code or authenticator app).
  5. Return to Stake. The browser returns to Stake automatically. Your wallet balance updates within 60 seconds. Check Neteller transaction history for the Neteller Transaction ID if you need to follow up.

Jak withdraw from Stake to Neteller

  1. Complete Stake identity verification. Submit a government-issued photo ID before your first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Neteller.
  3. Enter your Neteller account email and the withdrawal amount. The email must match the Neteller účet you want to receive funds in.
  4. Confirm on the summary screen. Double-check the email address.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the withdrawal in 15 minutes to 3 hours. Funds appear in your Neteller wallet balance immediately upon receipt. From there, transfer to your bank or use the Net+ card.

Limity a lhůty

Neteller's monthly upload limit for unverified účets is 2,500 EUR. Verified účets (with submitted ID and proof of address) have higher limits, potentially up to 1,000,000 EUR annually. For Stake withdrawals to Neteller, the effective limit is whichever is smaller: Stake's configured withdrawal maximum or your Neteller účet's available balance capacity.

Neteller processes transfers using the Paysafe Group infrastructure, which operates 24/7. Vklads are instant. Výběral timing from Stake to Neteller is determined by Stake's payment queue, not Neteller's processing speed. Once Stake dispatches the transfer, Neteller credits it immediately.

Neteller's Net+ prepaid Mastercard allows spending of Neteller balance at any Mastercard-accepting merchant worldwide. This gives you a direct path from Stake winnings to spendable funds: Stake withdrawal to Neteller (instant once dispatched), then spend via Net+ card.
