Jak funguje Sugar Rush 1000

Sugar Rush 1000 runs on a 7x7 grid using cluster pays rather than paylines. A cluster is 5 or more matching symbols touching horizontally or vertically. When a cluster forms, the involved symbols are removed and new ones cascade from above in the tumble mechanic. Consecutive cascades within one spin continue until no new cluster forms.

The name references the 1000x minimum volná točení multiplier mechanic, which distinguishes this from the original Sugar Rush. The grid is filled with candy symbols of varying values. Low-pay symbols include pink and yellow candies; premium symbols include lollipops, gummy bears, and the chocolate bar. The chocolate bar is the highest-paying symbol.

Multiplier symbols appear on the grid and function as modifiers. These apply to any cluster win that touches them. In the base game, multipliers increase the win value for individual cluster hits. The 7x7 grid allows for more complex cluster formations than a 6x5 grid, creating more tumble cascade opportunities.

Scatter symbols (the wrapped candy) trigger the volná točení feature. Very high volatility means the base game frequently delivers minimal cluster wins over extended spin sequences. The 7x7 grid and cluster mechanic create large symbol sets, spreading probability across more positions.

Bonus features

Four scatters trigger 10 volná točení. Five scatters award 12 volná točení. During volná točení, a global multiplier starts at 1x and increases by 1 each time a tumble occurs. The multiplier never resets during a single volná točení session. The minimum value of this multiplier at the end of any volná točení session is 1,000x applied to any final win: this is the 1000 in the game's name.

Kumulovaný globální multiplikátor může dosáhnout daleko za 1 000x, pokud jsou kaskádové řetězce dlouhé a četné. V sezení s mnoha po sobě jdoucími tumbles multiplikátor rychle stoupá. Když pod velkým kumulovaným multiplikátorem přistane cluster s vysokou hodnotou, strop 25 000x se stává matematicky dosažitelným.

Bonus buy is available at 100x stake for direct volná točení access. The RTP on bonus buy is comparable to the base game's 96.51%. Retriggers can extend the volná točení count during the feature. The 25,000x max win is Pragmatic Play's highest tier, placing Sugar Rush 1000 in the same ceiling category as Razor Shark from Push Gaming.

Strategy and session tipy

A 25,000x max win ceiling combined with very high volatility places Sugar Rush 1000 at the extreme end of variance. This requires the largest relative session bankroll of the Pragmatic Play titles in this recenze set. A 200-300 spin session budget at your chosen stake is a minimum for meaningful exposure to the feature.

The global multiplier mechanic during volná točení means the value of a single volná točení trigger is highly variable. A feature that runs only 10 spins with few cascades may produce a 1,000x total return (the floor guarantee on the multiplier), while a cascade-rich session could push toward five or six figures.

RTP of 96.51% is the same as Sweet Bonanza, but the variance distribution is wider due to the higher max win ceiling. Treat each spin as a low-probability event generating the house edge, not a guaranteed fractional return. For casual players, lower-volatility options with similar RTP are more appropriate. Sugar Rush 1000 suits players specifically targeting the volná točení multiplier mechanic.

The 1000x minimum multiplier guarantee during volná točení is a mathematically significant feature. It establishes a floor on any volná točení session: regardless of how few cascades occur, the multiplier will read at least 1000x when applied to the final win. This floor guarantee means even a modest final win during volná točení is multiplied by 1000 at minimum. The practical effect is that Sugar Rush 1000 volná točení sessions are less likely to produce near-zero returns than features without a floor multiplier.
