Jak funguje Razor Shark

Razor Shark is a 5x4 grid slot from Push Gaming on 20 fixed paylines. It is one of Push Gaming's most recognisable titles, built around an underwater shark theme with a distinctive sand reveal mechanic on reel 5.

Sada symbolů používá obrazy mořského života. Nízkohodnotné symboly jsou čtyři druhy ryb v rostoucích hodnotových vrstvách. Žralok je nejvýše hodnoceným symbolem. Standardní symboly wild nahrazují všechny běžné symboly. Charakteristická vizuální mechanika je písečná duna v dolní části obrazovky, která zakrývá dolní řady zobrazení válce, dokud symboly nepřistanou a vrstva písku není tlačena dolů.

The push mechanic on reel 5 is central to gameplay: mystery symbols land on reel 5 and are pushed down progressively. When a mystery symbol lands on reel 5, it reveals a multiplier value for the spin. These mystery push symbols appear in stacks on reel 5, increasing in multiplier value as the stack is pushed lower.

Scatter symbols (the scuba diver) trigger the volná točení feature. The base game already includes the mystery multiplier mechanic, making it more volatile than typical payline sloty. The 20-line structure supports both line wins and the multiplier overlay.

Bonus features

Three, four, or five scatter symbols trigger the volná točení round. Three scatters award 10 volná točení; four award 14; five award 18. During volná točení, the push mechanic continues with enhanced frequency. A special super spin mechanic activates when the mystery push stack is pushed to the bottom of reel 5: all reels spin simultaneously with the accumulated multiplier applying to the resulting win.

The volná točení feature can retrigger by landing 3 or more scatters during the feature, adding extra spins to the remaining count. The maximum multiplier accumulation during volná točení is the mechanism behind the 25,000x ceiling. Sustained push events during volná točení, with the super spin trigger activating under a high accumulated multiplier and premium symbols landing on multiple lines, produces the extreme win events.

Bonus buy at 100x stake provides direct volná točení access. Push Gaming's RTP for the bonus buy version is comparable to the base 96.70% RTP. The bonus buy is particularly effective for players focused on experiencing the push mechanic's peak behaviour in the volná točení context rather than the base game.
