Spacemanの仕組み

Spaceman replaces the aircraft used in Aviator-style games with an astronaut who floats upward from the bottom of the screen. A multiplier counter starts at 1.00x the moment betting closes and increases continuously as the astronaut ascends. You must press cashout before the astronaut "flies away," which ends the round with all uncashed bets losing.

Pragmatic Play allows two simultaneous bets per player, each with its own independent stake and cashout controls. You can set one bet to auto-cashout at a low target (for example, 1.5x) while leaving the second to manual judgment, effectively splitting your risk within a single round.

The pacing of Spaceman rounds tends to be slightly slower than some other crash titles, giving a brief window between round end and the next betting phase. The multiplier curve is not linear; early multiplier gains are fast, and reaching extreme values requires the round to run for an extended time.
