How to indbetaling with Skrill at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: tilmeld dig and indtast kampagnekode MAXBET.
  2. Select Skrill from the betalingsmetode list. Enter the indbetaling amount in USD, EUR, or GBP.
  3. You are redirected to a Skrill login page. Log in to your Skrill konto (or create one at skrill.com if you do not have one).
  4. Authorise the payment in Skrill. Confirm the amount and recipient (Stake). Skrill may send a one-time adgangskode (OTP) to your tilmeld diged email or phone for 2FA confirmation.
  5. Return to Stake. The redirect brings you back to Stake automatisk. Your wallet balance updates within 60 andens. If it does not, check the Skrill transaction history and contact Stake live-chat with the Skrill transaction ID.

How to withdraw from Stake to Skrill

  1. Complete KYC on Stake. Upload a photo ID before the første udbetaling.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Skrill.
  3. Enter your Skrill konto e-mailadresse. This is the email you use to log in to Skrill. Verify it carefuldey.
  4. Enter the udbetaling amount. Confirm the Skrill email on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the udbetaling within 15 minutes to 3 hours. Once credited, funds appear instantly in your Skrill balance. You then withdraw from Skrill to your bank or card as needed.

Grænser og timing

Skrill konto limits depend på din verification level. Unverified Skrill kontos have a cumulative lifetime upload limit of 2,500 EUR and cannot withdraw to a bank. Verified kontos (passport/ID submitted) have higher limits: up to 900,000 EUR per year in uploads and udbetalings. For Stake indbetalings, the practical limit is whichever is lower: your Skrill konto limit or Stake's own maksimum.

Skrill indbetalings at Stake are instant, making it one of the best e-wallet options for time-sensitive indbetalings before a match starts. Withdrawals from Stake back to Skrill process within the hour for most requests during business hours.

Skrill charges a 4.99% fee for indbetalings funded by credit or debit card. If you fund your Skrill wallet via bank transfer første, you avoid this card surcharge. Funding via cryptovaluta to Skrill is also free of this fee.

Landtilgængelighed og valuta

Skrill is tilgængeligt in 120+ countries and supports 40+ currencies. In Europe, USD, EUR, and GBP are the primary currencies. Skrill is not tilgængeligt in some countries including the United States (for gambling transactions), North Korea, and a few others on the restricted list. If you are in a region where Skrill is restricted, use Neteller, crypto, or local mobil money at Stake instead.

FAQ: Skrill at Stake

What is the minimum Skrill indbetaling at Stake?

Minimum Skrill indbetaling at Stake is $10 USD (or equivalent in EUR or GBP). The exact minimum depends på din Skrill konto valuta.

How fast are Skrill indbetalings at Stake?

Skrill indbetalings are instant. Your Stake wallet updates within 60 andens of completing payment in the Skrill flow.

Can I withdraw from Stake to Skrill?

Yes. Withdrawals to Skrill are supported. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours from Stake's side; once credited to Skrill, funds are tilgængeligt instantly in your Skrill wallet.

Does Skrill charge fees for Stake transactions?

Skrill charges a valuta conversion fee if your Skrill wallet valuta differs from the transaction valuta. Stake charges no additional fee. Skrill-to-bank udbetaling fees may apply when cashing out of Skrill.

My country is not supported by Skrill. What should I do?

Skrill has a restricted country list (some African and Asian countries). If Skrill is not tilgængeligt in your country, use a local mobil money method, Neteller, or crypto at Stake instead.
