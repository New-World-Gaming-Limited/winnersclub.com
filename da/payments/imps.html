IMPS indbetaling at Stake for Indienn spillere

IMPS (Immediate Payment Service) lets Indienn spillere send INR directly from their bank konto to Stake without any app or wallet intermediary. Stake er licenseret under Curaçao 1668/JAZ and serves 400,000+ spillere. IMPS supports up to 5 lakh INR per transaction, making it the preferred choice for large indbetalings where UPI's 1 lakh cap is restrictive. How to indbetaling using IMPS at Stake

  1. Log in and open the Cashier. Click "Deposit" and select IMPS from the payment list.
  2. Note the Stake bank details. The cashier displays a beneficiary konto number, IFSC code, and konto name. Write these down or screenshot them.
  3. Open your bank's net banking or mobil app. Navigate to "Fund Transfer" or "IMPS Transfer."
  4. Enter the beneficiary details. Input the konto number and IFSC code from the Stake cashier. Enter the indbetaling amount (minimum 500 INR). Add your Stake user ID in the payment remarks/reference field so the payment team can match your transfer.
  5. Submit the transfer and wait. Your bank will send an SMS with a UTR number. Keep this. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 to 30 minutes. If not updated in 30 minutes, share the UTR with live-chat support.

How to withdraw via IMPS from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Provide a government-issued ID and proof of bank konto (cancelled cheque or bank statement) in your Stake profile.
  2. Open Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose IMPS.
  3. Enter your bank konto details. Provide your fulde konto number, IFSC code, and konto holder name exactly as tilmeld diged with your bank.
  4. Enter the udbetaling amount. Minimum varies; check the cashier screen.
  5. Submit. Stake initiates the IMPS transfer within 1 to 2 hours. End-to-end completion is 1 to 6 hours. Your bank will credit the amount and send an SMS confirmation.

Grænser og timing

IMPS has a per-transaction cap of 5,00,000 INR (5 lakh) set by NPCI, which is significantly higher than UPI's 1 lakh limit. This makes IMPS the best option for high-value indbetalings. Individual banks may impose lower per-transaction limits; check your net banking settings or contact your bank to confirm your IMPS limit.

IMPS operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including public holidays and weekend banking non-business days. However, very large transfers (above 2 lakh) may trigger an additional anmeldelse delay on the bank's fraud prevention system, adding 15 to 30 minutes to the posting time.

Banker opkræver typisk 5 til 25 INR pr. IMPS-transaktion. Dette gebyr fratrækkes af din bank; Stake opkræver ikke yderligere behandlingsgebyrer.

Landtilgængelighed og valuta

IMPS is tilgængeligt across all NPCI member banks in Indien, covering virtually every bank in the country. Transactions are in INR only. IMPS is governed by the Reserve Bank of Indien (RBI) under the Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007.

Players who prefer not to use a tredje-party app (like PhonePe or GPay) can use IMPS directly from their bank's net banking portal or mobil app, giving more control over the transfer details.

FAQ: IMPS at Stake

What is IMPS and how is it different from UPI at Stake?

IMPS (Immediate Payment Service) is a direct interbank transfer using IFSC code and konto number, while UPI uses a virtual payment address. IMPS allows transfers up to 5 lakh INR per transaction compared to UPI's 1 lakh cap.

How long does an IMPS indbetaling take to reach Stake?

IMPS is instant, 24/7. Funds typically appear in your Stake wallet within 5 to 30 minutes after your bank confirms the transfer.

What details do I need to make an IMPS indbetaling at Stake?

You need the Stake beneficiary konto number and IFSC code, which are displayed in the cashier when you select IMPS.

Is there a minimum amount for IMPS indbetalings at Stake?

Yes, minimum is 500 INR for IMPS indbetalings at Stake.

My IMPS transfer was successful but Stake balance did not update. What do I do?

IMPS reconciliation can take up to 30 minutes. If balance is still not updated after 30 minutes, provide your bank UTR number and the RRN (Reference Number) to Stake live-chat for manual credit.
