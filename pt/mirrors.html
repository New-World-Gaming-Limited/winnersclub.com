Mirror vs VPN

Mirror sites have several clear advantages over VPN services for accessing Stake. A mirror is free, there is no subscription, no monthly fee, and no account required. Access is instant: you simply navigate to the mirror URL in your existing browser and you are on the platform. There is no software to install, no configuration, and no performance overhead. This latency can affect live betting, where odds change in fractions of a second, and crash games, where a delayed cash-out tap can cost you a winning position. Additionally, Stake's fraud detection systems may flag accounts that regularly access the platform from different countries via VPN, potentially triggering additional verification steps or temporary holds. VPNs themselves are also increasingly blocked in some jurisdictions, meaning the VPN service may stop working at any time.
