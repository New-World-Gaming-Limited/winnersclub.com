How to deposit Ethereum at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter promo code MAXBET.
  2. Select Ethereum (ETH) from the cryptocurrency deposit options. Ensure you select the correct network: Ethereum. Sending ETH on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake ETH deposit address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send ETH from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake deposit address, enter the amount (minimum 0.01 ETH), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmations. The Ethereum network requires 12 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. 2 to 5 minutes (12 Ethereum confirmations).

How to withdraw Ethereum from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto withdrawals.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Ethereum (ETH).
  3. Enter your ETH wallet address. This must be an address on the Ethereum network. Double-check the address character by character; crypto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Stake deducts a network fee from the amount to cover on-chain transaction costs. The fee is shown before you confirm.
  5. Submit and track the transaction. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the Ethereum network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Withdrawal processing takes 15 to 45 minutes after submission.

Limites e prazos para depósitos e saques em Ethereum

Ethereum deposits at Stake require 12 network confirmations. With Ethereum's 12-second block time post-Merge, this means deposits typically confirm in 2 to 4 minutes under normal network conditions. Track your deposit status on Etherscan.io with your transaction hash.

Ethereum gas fees are paid in ETH (gwei) and fluctuate with network usage. Standard ETH transfers cost less gas than complex smart contract calls. A simple ETH transfer typically costs 21,000 gas units; at 30 gwei gas price, that is 0.00063 ETH (approximately $2 to $3 USD at typical prices). During peak demand periods, gas prices can spike 10x.

No Stake maximum deposit cap for ETH. Minimum deposit is 0.01 ETH. Withdrawal network fees are deducted from the withdrawal amount and shown before confirmation.

FAQ: Ethereum at Stake

What is the minimum Ethereum deposit at Stake?

Minimum Ethereum deposit at Stake is 0.01 ETH (approximately ~$25 USD at current rates). The minimum changes if ETH price moves significantly; always check the cashier for the current minimum.

How long does a Ethereum deposit take to confirm?

2 to 5 minutes (12 Ethereum confirmations). Ethereum deposits require 12 network confirmations before crediting to your Stake wallet.

Does Stake charge a fee for Ethereum deposits or withdrawals?

Stake does not charge a deposit fee. For withdrawals, Stake deducts a network fee from the withdrawal amount to cover the blockchain transaction cost. Ethereum gas fees are paid in ETH and vary with network congestion; typically $2 to $20 USD.

Can I use Ethereum to avoid geographic restrictions on local banking?

Yes. ETH deposits and withdrawals are geographic-agnostic. Players in India, Pakistan, Africa, and other regions where local banking options are limited can use ETH without RBI, SBP, or local bank restrictions.

My Ethereum deposit has not arrived. What should I do?

Primeiro, confirme que a transação tem 12 confirmações no explorador blockchain do Ethereum usando o hash da sua transação (TXID). Se confirmada, entre em contato com o live-chat do Stake com o TXID. Se ainda não confirmada, aguarde a rede processá-la.
