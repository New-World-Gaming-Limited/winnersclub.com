How to deposit at Stake with MTN MoMo

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register with promo code MAXBET.
  2. Select MTN Mobile Money from the payment list. Enter the deposit amount in your local currency.
  3. Note the Stake MTN MoMo merchant number displayed on screen.
  4. Open the MTN MoMo app or dial *170# (Ghana) or *165# (Uganda). Select "Pay Bill," "Merchant Pay," or "Send Money." Enter the Stake merchant number, the amount, and your Stake user ID as the reference. Confirm with your MTN MoMo PIN.
  5. Wait for the SMS confirmation from MTN. Your Stake balance updates within 5 minutes. Keep the MTN transaction ID for reference.

How to withdraw from Stake to MTN MoMo

  1. Complete identity verification on Stake. Submit your national ID (Ghana Card, Ugandan National ID, or equivalent) before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose MTN MoMo.
  3. Enter your MTN registered phone number. Verify the number format: Ghana uses 024/054/055/059-XXXXXXX; Uganda uses 077/078-XXXXXXX.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Verify name match between MTN MoMo account and Stake registration.
  5. Submit. Funds arrive within 15 minutes to 3 hours. MTN sends an SMS confirming receipt.

Limites e prazos

MTN MoMo limits in Ghana (2024): per transaction up to GHS 10,000, daily up to GHS 10,000, monthly up to GHS 30,000. These limits apply to standard accounts. Enhanced accounts with biometric verification may have higher limits. Check current limits by dialling *170# in Ghana.

MTN MoMo limits in Uganda (2024): per transaction up to UGX 7,000,000, daily up to UGX 20,000,000 for registered accounts. Limits are set by Bank of Uganda and MTN Uganda together.

MTN MoMo operates 24/7. Deposits confirm in 2 to 5 minutes. Withdrawals from Stake typically process within an hour during operational hours. An overnight batch may process requests received after 10 PM local time the following morning.

Disponibilidade por país e moeda

MTN MoMo is available in 17 African countries with 238 million registered customers as of 2024. For Stake deposits, the primary supported currencies are GHS (Ghana), UGX (Uganda), and XOF/XAF (West/Central African CFA franc for Francophone markets). Stake's MoMo integration may not cover every country MTN operates in; verify your country in the cashier before depositing.

FAQ: MTN MoMo at Stake

Which countries can use MTN MoMo at Stake?

MTN MoMo is available in Ghana (GHS), Uganda (UGX), Cameroon, Ivory Coast, Benin, Burkina Faso, Guinea, Rwanda, South Africa, Zambia, and Eswatini. Check the Stake cashier for your country's availability.

What is the minimum MTN MoMo deposit at Stake?

Minimum MTN MoMo deposit is GHS 5 in Ghana or UGX 2,000 in Uganda. Minimums vary by country.

How fast is an MTN MoMo deposit at Stake?

MTN MoMo deposits are processed in real time. Expect your Stake balance to update within 2 to 5 minutes after confirming the payment.

Does Stake support MTN MoMo withdrawals?

Yes. MTN MoMo withdrawals are supported. Enter your MTN registered phone number in the withdrawal form. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours.

MTN MoMo payment failed but money was deducted. What happens?

MTN MoMo auto-reverses failed payments within 24 hours. If you do not see a reversal after 24 hours, contact MTN MoMo customer care with the transaction ID. Also contact Stake live-chat to check if the payment was received.
