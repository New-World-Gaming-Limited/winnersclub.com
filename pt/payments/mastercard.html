How to deposit with Mastercard at Stake

  1. Enable online/international transactions. Open your bank app and confirm your Mastercard has online e-commerce and international transactions enabled. Set an appropriate daily limit.
  2. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Register if new; enter promo code MAXBET.
  3. Select Mastercard (or Card) from payment options. Enter the deposit amount.
  4. Enter card details. 16-digit card number, expiry date, CVV (3 digits on back), and cardholder name as printed.
  5. Complete 3D Secure (Mastercard Identity Check). An OTP or push notification from your bank arrives. Approve within the time limit. Your Stake wallet updates within 5 minutes.

How to withdraw to Mastercard from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Submit photo ID and card ownership proof before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Check whether card withdrawal is listed.
  3. If available, select Mastercard. Enter card number and withdrawal amount. Submit.
  4. Await the refund. Card withdrawals take 3 to 5 business days to appear due to Mastercard's refund processing timeline. This is not a Stake delay.
  5. If card withdrawal is not listed, use a bank transfer or e-wallet option in the cashier instead.

Limites e prazos

Mastercard deposit limits at Stake are determined by your issuing bank. Most banks set daily online purchase limits between $1,000 and $5,000 USD for standard cards; premium and infinite cards may have higher limits. Check your bank app under "Card Limits" or call your bank to confirm.

For Indian players: Indian banks often apply gaming MCC (7995) blocks to both Visa and Mastercard. If your Mastercard is declined, UPI is the most reliable alternative for INR deposits. Crypto (Bitcoin, USDT TRC20) works without any MCC classification and is available regardless of which bank issued your card.

3D Secure (Mastercard Identity Check) is required for all Mastercard transactions at Stake. This is a security layer that protects your card from unauthorized use. Ensure your bank has your current phone number for OTP delivery.

Foreign transaction fees from your bank (typically 1.5% to 3%) apply if your card currency and the transaction currency differ. FAQ: Mastercard at Stake

Does Stake accept Mastercard debit and credit cards?

Yes. Stake accepts Mastercard debit, credit, and prepaid cards. Online transactions must be enabled on your card before depositing.

What is the minimum Mastercard deposit at Stake?

Minimum Mastercard deposit at Stake is $10 USD or local currency equivalent.

My Mastercard was declined at Stake. Why?

Common reasons: gaming MCC block by your bank, exceeded daily limit, failed 3D Secure OTP, or international transaction restriction. Enable international transactions in your banking app and retry. If declined again, use crypto or an e-wallet.

Can I withdraw from Stake to my Mastercard?

Card withdrawals to Mastercard may be available; check the cashier. If not, use bank transfer or an e-wallet for withdrawals.

Does Stake charge extra for Mastercard deposits?

Stake does not charge any fee for Mastercard deposits. Your bank may charge a foreign transaction fee if the deposit currency differs from your card currency.
