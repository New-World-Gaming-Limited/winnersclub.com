How to depósito with Neteller at Stake

  1. Inicia sesión to Stake and open the Cashier. Regístrate first if new; enter código promocional NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Neteller from the payment options. Enter the depósito amount in USD or EUR.
  3. Inicia sesión to Neteller. You are redirected to a Neteller-hosted page. Enter your Neteller cuenta email and secure ID.
  4. Approve the payment. Confirm the merchant name (Stake) and amount. Complete any 2FA required by Neteller (email code or authenticator app).
  5. Return to Stake. The browser returns to Stake automatically. Your wallet balance updates within 60 seconds. Check Neteller transaction history for the Neteller Transaction ID if you need to follow up.

How to withdraw from Stake to Neteller

  1. Complete Stake identity verification. Submit a government-issued photo ID before your first retiro.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retiro. Choose Neteller.
  3. Enter your Neteller cuenta email and the retiro amount. The email must match the Neteller cuenta you want to receive funds in.
  4. Confirm on the summary screen. Double-check the email address.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the retiro in 15 minutos to 3 horas. Funds appear in your Neteller wallet balance immediately upon receipt. From there, transfer to your bank or use the Net+ card.

Límites y tiempos

Neteller's mensual upload limit for unverified cuentas is 2,500 EUR. Verified cuentas (with submitted ID and proof of address) have higher limits, potentially up to 1,000,000 EUR annually. For Stake retiros to Neteller, the effective limit is whichever is smaller: Stake's configured retiro maximum or your Neteller cuenta's available balance capacity.

Neteller processes transfers using the Paysafe Group infrastructure, which operates 24/7. Depositas are instant. Retiro timing from Stake to Neteller is determined by Stake's payment queue, not Neteller's processing speed. Once Stake dispatches the transfer, Neteller credits it immediately.

Neteller's Net+ prepaid Mastercard allows spending of Neteller balance at any Mastercard-accepting merchant worldwide. This gives you a direct path from Stake winnings to spendable funds: Stake retiro to Neteller (instant once dispatched), then spend via Net+ card.

Disponibilidad por país y moneda

FAQ: Neteller at Stake

What is the minimum Neteller depósito at Stake?

Minimum Neteller depósito at Stake is $10 USD or EUR equivalent.

How quickly are Neteller depósitos credited at Stake?

Neteller depósitos are instant. Your Stake wallet updates within 60 seconds of authorising the payment in Neteller.

Can I get a Net+ card to use my Stake Neteller winnings in person?

Yes. Neteller issues the Net+ prepaid Mastercard linked to your Neteller wallet. Once winnings are withdrawn from Stake to Neteller, you can use the Net+ card at any Mastercard terminal.

Does Stake support Neteller retiros?
