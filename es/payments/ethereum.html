How to depósito Ethereum at Stake

  1. Inicia sesión to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter código promocional NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Ethereum (ETH) from the criptomoneda depósito options. Ensure you select the correct network: Ethereum. Sending ETH on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake ETH depósito address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send ETH from your cripto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake depósito address, enter the amount (minimum 0.01 ETH), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmations. The Ethereum network requires 12 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. 2 to 5 minutos (12 Ethereum confirmations).

How to withdraw Ethereum from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large cripto retiros.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retiro. Choose Ethereum (ETH).
  3. Enter your ETH wallet address. This must be an address on the Ethereum network. Double-check the address character by character; cripto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Enter the retiro amount. Stake deducts a network fee from the amount to cover on-chain transaction costs. The fee is shown before you confirm.
  5. Submit and track the transaction. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the Ethereum network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Retiro processing takes 15 to 45 minutos after submission.

Límites y tiempos for Ethereum depósitos and retiros

Ethereum depósitos at Stake require 12 network confirmations. With Ethereum's 12-second block time post-Merge, this means depósitos typically confirm in 2 to 4 minutos under normal network conditions. Track your depósito status on Etherscan.io with your transaction hash.

Ethereum gas fees are paid in ETH (gwei) and fluctuate with network usage. Standard ETH transfers cost less gas than complex smart contract calls. A simple ETH transfer typically costs 21,000 gas units; at 30 gwei gas price, that is 0.00063 ETH (approximately $2 to $3 USD at typical prices). During peak demand periods, gas prices can spike 10x.

No Stake maximum depósito cap for ETH. Minimum depósito is 0.01 ETH. Retiro network fees are deducted from the retiro amount and shown before confirmation.

FAQ: Ethereum at Stake

What is the minimum Ethereum depósito at Stake?

Minimum Ethereum depósito at Stake is 0.01 ETH (approximately ~$25 USD at current rates). The minimum changes if ETH price moves significantly; always check the cashier for the current minimum.

How long does a Ethereum depósito take to confirm?

2 to 5 minutos (12 Ethereum confirmations). Ethereum depósitos require 12 network confirmations before crediting to your Stake wallet.

Does Stake charge a fee for Ethereum depósitos or retiros?

Stake no cobra comisión de depósito. Para los retiros, Stake deduce una comisión de red del monto del retiro para cubrir el costo de la transacción en blockchain. Ethereum gas fees are paid in ETH and vary with network congestion; typically $2 to $20 USD.

Can I use Ethereum to avoid geographic restrictions on local banking?

Yes. ETH depósitos and retiros are geographic-agnostic. Los jugadores en India, Pakistan, Africa, and other regions where local banking options are limited can use ETH without RBI, SBP, or local bank restrictions.

My Ethereum depósito has not arrived. What should I do?

Primero, confirma que la transacción tenga 12 confirmaciones en el explorador de blockchain de Ethereum usando tu hash de transacción (TXID). Si está confirmada, contacta al chat en vivo de Stake con el TXID. Si aún no está confirmada, espera a que la red la procese.
