How to depósito using NEFT at Stake

  1. Inicia sesión and open the Cashier. Select "Deposita," then choose NEFT from the método de pago list.
  2. Copy the Stake beneficiary details. The cashier displays the cuenta number, IFSC code, and cuenta name. Screenshot this screen.
  3. Inicia sesión to your bank's net banking or aplicación móvil. Add Stake as a NEFT beneficiary using the details from step 2. Beneficiary addition may require a 30-minute bank cool-down on first-time additions.
  4. Initiate the NEFT transfer. Select NEFT, choose the Stake beneficiary, enter the amount (minimum 500 INR), and include your Stake user ID in the payment remarks field. Confirm with your net banking password or OTP.
  5. Wait for the batch to settle. NEFT batches run every 30 minutos. You will get a UTR confirmation SMS from your bank. Your Stake wallet updates after the batch settles, typically within 30 to 120 minutos. Keep the UTR in case you need to follow up with support.

How to withdraw via NEFT from Stake

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Upload PAN card or Aadhaar and a bank proof document before your first retiro.
  2. Open Cashier and click Withdraw. Select NEFT.
  3. Enter your bank cuenta number, IFSC code, and cuenta holder name. These must match your bank records exactly.
  4. Enter the retiro amount. NEFT retiros have no system-level cap, but Stake's risk team may request additional verification for very large retiros.
  5. Submit. Stake queues your retiro request. NEFT credits arrive in 2 to 12 horas depending on when the next batch settles at your bank. Your bank sends an SMS on credit.

Límites y tiempos

RBI removed the NEFT per-transaction ceiling in December 2019, meaning there is no cap on individual NEFT transfers at the network level. However, your bank may still impose diario transfer limits through net banking (common caps are 5 lakh to 25 lakh per day depending on the bank and cuenta type). Check your bank's net banking settings under "Transaction Limits" to see your specific cap.

NEFT operates 24/7 on all days of the year including Sundays and public holidays since December 2019. Batches settle every 30 minutos, starting at midnight. If you initiate a transfer at 2:15 PM, the batch settles at 2:30 PM and Stake should receive the funds by 3:00 PM at the latest.

NEFT es más lento que IMPS o UPI para montos pequeños, pero para transferencias superiores a 5 lakh sigue siendo una opción confiable sin las alertas bancarias ocasionales de IMPS en montos altos.

Disponibilidad por país y moneda

NEFT is available at all banks that are RBI members in India, covering virtually all public and private sector banks, regional rural banks, and cooperative banks. NEFT transfers are in INR only. Stake's receiving bank cuenta is an Indian bank cuenta denominated in INR.

Non-resident Indians (NRI) with NRE or NRO cuentas can also use NEFT if their bank allows outward domestic transfers from these cuentas.

FAQ: NEFT at Stake

How long does a NEFT depósito take at Stake?

NEFT processes in half-hourly batches 24/7. Depositas typically credit to your Stake wallet within 30 to 120 minutos after the bank initiates the transfer.

Is there a maximum limit on NEFT depósitos at Stake?

RBI has removed the NEFT transaction cap. Theoretically you can transfer any amount. In practice, your bank may impose diario or per-transaction limits; check your net banking settings.

What is the minimum NEFT depósito at Stake?

Minimum NEFT depósito is 500 INR.

Does Stake charge a fee for NEFT depósitos?

Stake charges no fee. Your bank may charge up to 25 INR for outward NEFT transfers depending on your cuenta type.

My NEFT depósito has not arrived after 2 horas. What should I do?

Collect your bank's NEFT UTR number and the transfer acknowledgement. Contacto Stake live-chat and provide both the UTR and your Stake user ID. The payments team can locate and manually post the credit.
