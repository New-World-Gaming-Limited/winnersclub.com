How to depósito with a Visa card at Stake

  1. Enable online transactions on your card. Before attempting a card depósito, confirm in your bank's app that online/international transactions are enabled. Set your diario online limit to cover the intended depósito amount.
  2. Inicia sesión to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter código promocional NEWBONUS.
  3. Select Visa (or Card) from the payment options. Enter the depósito amount and click Proceed.
  4. Enter your Visa card details. Card number (16 digits), expiry month/year, CVV (3-digit code on the back). Name on card as printed.
  5. Complete 3D Secure verification. Your bank sends an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP within the time limit (typically 3 minutos). The transaction is approved, and your Stake wallet updates within 5 minutos.

How to withdraw to a Visa card from Stake

  1. Complete KYC verification. Submit a photo ID and proof that the card belongs to you (a photo of the card with the middle 8 digits obscured).
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retiro. Check if card retiro is available.
  3. If available, select Visa. Enter the card number and the retiro amount.
  4. Submit. Card refunds typically take 3 to 5 business days to appear in your bank statement. This is a Visa network timeline, not a Stake delay.
  5. If card retiro is not available, use an e-wallet or bank transfer as the retiro method instead.

Límites y tiempos

Visa card limits at Stake are controlled by your issuing bank, not Stake. Check your bank's online transaction diario limit in your banking app. Standard Visa debit cards typically allow $1,000 to $5,000 USD per day online; Visa credit cards may have higher limits depending on your credit limit.

Note for Indian players: Some Indian banks block gaming merchant category code (MCC 7995) transactions on Visa cards. If your Visa card is declined, use UPI, IMPS, or cripto (Bitcoin, USDT TRC20) as alternatives that bypass MCC restrictions entirely. These options are unaffected by bank MCC filtering.

3D Secure is mandatory for all Visa card transactions at Stake. Ensure your bank has your current mobile number registered to receive OTP. If your OTP does not arrive within 60 seconds, request a resend or contact your bank.

FAQ: Visa cards at Stake

What types of Visa cards are accepted at Stake?

Stake accepts Visa debit, Visa credit, and Visa prepaid cards. The card must have online transactions enabled and sufficient available credit or balance.

Why was my Visa card declined at Stake?

Common reasons: your bank blocks gaming merchant category code (MCC 7995), your card's online transaction limit is exceeded, 3D Secure OTP failed, or the card issuer requires manual approval for international transactions. Try a different card, use UPI (India) or cripto as alternatives.

Can I withdraw to my Visa card from Stake?

Visa card retiros are available at some Stake cuentas but not universally. Check the cashier retiro section for card retiro availability. If not available, withdraw via bank transfer or e-wallet instead.

What is the minimum Visa card depósito at Stake?

Minimum Visa card depósito at Stake is $10 USD (or local currency equivalent).

Indian Visa cards are being declined at Stake. What should I do?

Some Indian banks block transactions to international gaming merchants. Use UPI, IMPS, or cripto (Bitcoin, USDT TRC20) as reliable alternatives that bypass this MCC restriction.
