Depositao via PIX na Stake: como fazer (How to depósito with PIX at Stake)

  1. Crie sua conta / Regístrate your Stake cuenta. New users: complete the registration form and enter código promocional MAXBET to activate el bono de bienvenida.
  2. Acesse o Caixa (Open the Cashier). Click the green "Deposita" button. The cashier panel opens.
  3. Selecione PIX (Select PIX). Choose PIX from the método de pago list. Enter the BRL amount you want to depósito (minimum R$20).
  4. Escaneie o QR code (Scan the QR code). A PIX QR code and a copy-paste PIX key string appear on screen. Open your bank's app (Itau, Bradesco, Nubank, Caixa, BTG, or any PIX-enabled bank), navigate to "PIX," tap "Pagar via QR code," and scan the code. Alternatively, paste the PIX copy-paste key directly.
  5. Confirme o pagamento (Confirm). Your Stake balance updates in under 60 seconds. A confirmation email arrives shortly after. Keep the PIX end-to-end transaction ID (E2EID) from your bank app in case you need to contact support.

How to withdraw from Stake via PIX

  1. Complete cuenta verification. Submit CPF and a valid Brazilian ID (RG or CNH) in your Stake profile before the first retiro.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Retiro. Choose PIX.
  3. Enter your PIX chave (key). This is typically your CPF number, registered mobile number, or email address linked to your bank cuenta. PIX keys are case-sensitive when using email format.
  4. Enter the retiro amount. Check that the name on the PIX key matches your Stake-registered name; mismatches can delay or reject the transfer.
  5. Submit. Stake processes retiros within 30 to 60 minutos during business horas. PIX transfers complete in seconds on the banking side, so the total time is mostly queue-dependent on Stake's end.

Límites y tiempos

Banco Central do Brasil (BCB) sets PIX limits at the individual bank level. For natural persons, the default daytime limit is R$20,000 BRL per transaction and the overnight limit (8 PM to 6 AM) is lower, typically R$1,000 BRL. You can request a limit increase through your bank's app, usually effective within 24 horas.

PIX is available 24 horas a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Unlike TED (which requires bank business horas) or boleto (which requires manual processing), PIX has no blackout windows. This makes it ideal for late-night depósitos after a match or event you want to bet on.

CPF validation: PIX keys linked to a CPF are validated by the DICT (Diretorio de Identificadores de Contas Transacionais) system. If the CPF on the PIX key does not match the name registered on your Stake cuenta, the retiro may be returned. Ensure your Stake registration name exactly matches your bank cuenta name.

Disponibilidad por país y moneda

PIX is available in Brazil only, for individuals and businesses registered with Brazilian financial institutions. All transactions are in BRL. Stake's PIX integration processes payments in real time through a Brazilian payment aggregator regulated by BCB.

Brazilian residents can create a PIX key using any of four key types: CPF/CNPJ, mobile phone number, email address, or an EVP (Evolutionary Virtual Key) generated by their bank. Any of these key types are accepted at Stake for retiros.
