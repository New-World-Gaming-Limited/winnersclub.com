Cómo funciona esta calculadora

Todas las cuotas de apuestas son solo diferentes formas de expresar la misma probabilidad subyacente. The odds converter takes any single format as input and derives a decimal representation first, then converts outward to every other format using the exact mathematical relationships between them.

Decimal odds represent total return per unit staked, including your stake. Una cuota de 2.50 significa que una apuesta de 1 unidad devuelve 2.50 unidades en total (1.50 de ganancia). This is the native format at Stake and most European sportsbooks.

Fractional odds are written as numerator/denominator (e.g. 6/4). Profit equals stake multiplied by numerator/denominator. A 6/4 bet on a 10-unit stake yields 15 units profit.

American (moneyline) odds use positive numbers for underdogs and negative numbers for favorites. +150 significa que una apuesta de 100 unidades gana 150 de ganancia. -200 significa que necesitas apostar 200 para ganar 100 de ganancia.

Hong Kong odds equal decimal odds minus 1 (just the profit per unit). Un decimal de 2.50 equivale a Hong Kong 1.50.

Indonesian odds work like American but scaled per unit: positive values show profit per unit staked; negative values show how much you stake per unit of profit.

Malay odds range from -1 to +1. Positive values represent profit per unit; negative values represent required stake per unit of profit. A Malay of -0.67 approximates American -150.

The implied probability formula is: probability = 1 / decimal odds. A price of 2.50 carries 40% implied probability. Comparing this figure against your own assessed probability tells you whether a bet has positive expected value.

Todas las conversiones son sin pérdida dentro de la precisión de punto flotante. Fractional odds are approximated to the nearest standard fraction when displaying (e.g. 1.5/1 rounds to 3/2), though the calculator uses the exact decimal internally for all outputs.

FAQ

What is the most common odds format?

Las cuotas decimales son el estándar global usado en Europa, Australia y en Stake. Las cuotas fraccionarias siguen siendo populares en el Reino Unido. American (moneyline) odds dominate North American markets.

How do I convert decimal to fractional odds?

Resta 1 del decimal, luego exprésalo como fracción en términos más bajos. Decimal 2.50 becomes (2.50-1)/1 = 3/2 (written as 3/2 or "3 to 2").

What does implied probability mean?

La probabilidad implícita es el porcentaje de probabilidad a bookmaker's price implies for an outcome. It equals 1 divided by the decimal odds. If the market prices an outcome at 2.00, the implied probability is 50%.

How are American odds converted to decimal?

Para cuotas americanas positivas: decimal = (Americana / 100) + 1. Para negativas: decimal = (100 / |Americana|) + 1. Entonces +200 = 3.00 decimal y -200 = 1.50 decimal.

What is the overround and why does it matter?

El overround (también llamado vig o juice) es la suma de todas las probabilidades implícitas en un mercado menos el 100%. A 5% overround means the book keeps roughly 5 cents per dollar wagered. Lower overround means better value for the bettor.
