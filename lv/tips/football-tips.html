How we lietotneroach football tips

A useful football tip starts with a clear question: why does this line exist, and why is the bookmaker pricing it where it is? Our process begins four to six hours before kick-off, when team jaunss is largely confirmed and the derības tirgus has absorbed the early sharp money.

Form is the pirmais filter. Most reliable football tirguss

Not every tirgus is equally liquid or equally predictable. Below are the five tirguss that consistently return the strongest results across a pilnīgs season of tipping.

When both sides average more than 1.2 goals scored and more than 0.9 goals conceded per match, BTTS Yes closes above 60 percent on a long sample. Bankrols for tipping

A tip with a strong rationale is only useful if the stake behind it is sized sensibly. Flat staking is the simplest method: decide on a unit size (1 to 2 percent of your kopā bankroll per tip) and lietotnely it to every selection regardless of confidence level.
