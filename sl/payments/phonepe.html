Kako deposit with PhonePe at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake. If you do not have an račun, register and enter MAXBET to activate the bonus dobrodošlice.
  2. Open the Cashier. Tap the "Polog" button in the top navigation. The payment panel slides open.
  3. Select PhonePe. From the list of payment options, choose PhonePe. Input the INR amount (minimum 300 INR).
  4. Complete the PhonePe payment. You will be shown a QR code or a UPI deep link. If deep link, it will open the PhonePe app directly. Approve the payment using your PhonePe UPI PIN. If using QR, open PhonePe, tap "Scan QR," and scan the displayed code.
  5. Confirm the balance update. Return to Stake. Your wallet should reflect the deposit in under 60 seconds. Nete the PhonePe transaction ID from your app in case you need to raise a query.

Kako withdraw via PhonePe from Stake

  1. Verify your identity. Complete KYC (government ID upload) in your Stake Profile before your first withdrawal.
  2. Go to the Cashier and click Withdraw.
  3. Select PhonePe (UPI). Enter your PhonePe VPA (example: 9XXXXXXXXX@ybl or yourname@axisbank).
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Check the minimum withdrawal requirement shown on screen. Confirm the VPA is correct.
  5. Submit the request. The amount debits from your Stake wallet immediately. Funds arrive in your bank račun linked to PhonePe within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS from PhonePe confirms credit.

Omejitve in roki

PhonePe transactions run on NPCI's UPI network, which enforces a 1,00,000 INR per-transaction ceiling. There is also a per-day cap across all UPI apps on a single bank račun, typically 1,00,000 INR aggregated. If you approach this limit, you will need to wait until midnight for the counter to reset, or use a secondary UPI-linked račun.

Lastna denarnica PhonePe (ločena od UPI) ima nižje omejitve: 10.000 INR na transakcijo in 20.000 INR mesečno za denarnice brez KYC. Stake sprejema tok UPI VPA, ne stanje denarnice PhonePe, zato veljajo višje omejitve NPCI.

Dvigals at Stake follow a standard queue. First-time withdrawals within a 30-day window may take up to 4 hours due to compliance checks. Subsequent withdrawals in the same month typically process within 30 to 90 minutes.

FAQ: PhonePe at Stake

Does Stake support direct PhonePe deposits?

Da. PhonePe is available in the Stake cashier as a dedicated option. It uses the UPI rail, so the flow is the same as any UPI payment but pre-selects the PhonePe app.

What is the minimum PhonePe deposit at Stake?

Minimum is 300 INR, consistent with UPI limits set by NPCI.

Can I withdraw to my PhonePe wallet from Stake?

Dvigals go to the bank račun linked to your PhonePe VPA. Direct wallet credit is not supported; the amount lands in your savings račun.

PhonePe shows payment failed but money was deducted. What now?

Do not retry immediately. Open Stake live-chat, provide your PhonePe transaction ID (found in PhonePe transaction history). Most auto-reversals complete within 5 minutes; manual credit takes up to 2 hours.

Is PhonePe available 24/7 for Stake deposits?

Da. PhonePe operates 24/7 on the UPI network. Even on bank holidays, the NPCI switch routes the transaction to your bank's UPI service.
