Kako deluje Aviatrix

Aviatrix follows the established crash-game format: a plane appears, a multiplier counter begins at 1.00x and rises, and you must cashout before the plane flies away. The game ends instantly when the plane disappears, and any uncashed bets are lost. The distinguishing feature of Aviatrix is the social layer built on top of this core mechanic.

A live leaderboard displays the top cashouts achieved by all active players in the current session. A shared bet feed shows every bet placed and every cashout executed in real time, which gives a sense of community activity without revealing strategic information about upcoming crash points.

NFT plane skins allow you to customise the aircraft with unique visual identifiers. These are purely cosmetic: the RNG determines the crash point independently of which plane skin is active. Skins can be acquired, traded, or held as blockchain assets outside the game, but their presence or absence has no effect on your expected value.

Each round uses a provably fair system. The server seed hash is publicly shown before betting opens. After the round, the seed is revealed and can be combined with your client seed in a publicly documented algorithm to reproduce the crash point. This confirms that the crash point was fixed before any bets were placed.

Strategija in bankroll za Aviatrix

Aviatrix's 97% RTP matches JetX and the Spribe titles, making it one of the more player-favourable crash options at Stake. The social features are engaging but can create misleading impressions: when you see another player predčasna izplačilo at 10x on the leaderboard, that result has no predictive value for the next round.

Avoiding social-proof bias: The public bet feed shows large wins prominently. This is a display feature, not a signal about upcoming rounds. Resist the temptation to increase your stake or cashout target based on what other players are doing. Each round is independent.

Low-multiplier auto-cashout: A 2x auto-cashout in Aviatrix targets a break-even success rate of approximately 48.5% at 97% RTP. This is a disciplined, low-variance approach for players who want defined, repeatable outcomes. Use a flat stake of 1-2% of session bankroll per round. Accept that losing streaks of 4-6 rounds will occur during normal play.

Targeting medium multipliers: Setting auto-cashout between 3x and 8x reduces win frequency but increases the payout per successful round. With a 97% RTP, the expected return per bet is constant regardless of target multiplier over a large sample, but variance increases with target size. Reduce stake size proportionally.

Big-play sessions: Going for 25x, 50x, or 100x requires staking very small amounts per round. A realistic approach is 0.25-0.5% of bankroll per round at these targets. The probability of hitting 100x in a single session with a reasonable number of rounds is low; treat it as entertainment expenditure rather than an income strategy.

Provably fair verification in Aviatrix

Aviatrix uporablja kriptografski sistem z dvojnim semenom. Strežnik pred vsakim krogom ustvari naključno seme in objavi njegovo zgoščeno vrednost. Zagotovite seme odjemalca. Točka padca se izpelje iz kombinacije obeh semen z dokumentiranim algoritmom, kar pomeni, da nobena stran po objavi zgoščene vrednosti ne more spremeniti rezultata.

After each round, the server seed is revealed. You can hash it independently to confirm it matches the pre-round disclosure, then apply the derivation algorithm to confirm the crash point. Aviatrix provides a verification interface within the game and also documents the algorithm publicly for independent checking.
