Three rooms to know

Crash (99% RTP)

The flagship. A multiplier ticks up from 1.00× until it crashes. Cash out before the crash to bank stake × multiplier. Hard $1M payout cap per round. Provably fair via SHA-256 hash chain. Strictly better odds than Aviator at 97%.

Plinko (99% RTP)

Drop a ball through a triangular peg field. Three risk profiles (Low / Medium / High) and 8/12/14/16 row counts. The high-risk 16-row board has a 1,000× top multiplier on the outer slots, and a heavy 0.5× weight in the centre.

Mines (99% RTP)

A 5×5 grid. You set the number of mines (1 to 24). Reveal safe tiles to compound the multiplier. Cash out at any point. With 24 mines on the board, hitting one safe tile pays 24.75×. With one mine, you can theoretically run to 24,000× by clearing 24 tiles.

Limbo (99% RTP)

Set a target multiplier (e.g. 5×). The game generates a random multiplier. If it exceeds your target, you win stake × target. The lower the chance of success, the larger the win. Max payout: 1,000,000×.

Dice (99% RTP)

Predict the next dice roll above or below a chosen threshold (between 0.01 and 99.99). Lower probability, higher multiplier. Used heavily by the auto-bet community.

Wheel (96-99% RTP)

A segmented wheel with selectable risk level (Low/Med/High) and segment count (10/20/30/40/50). High-risk 50-segment wheel pays up to 49.5× on a single hit.

Provably fair

Every Stake Original uses an open SHA-256 commit-reveal scheme. Before a round is played, the server publishes a hashed seed. Your client seed and a nonce are combined with the server seed to produce the round outcome. After the round ends, Stake reveals the server seed so you can independently verify that the result was determined before you placed your bet, not after.

Inside the in-game Fairness tab you can rotate your client seed any time. After a rotation, the previous server seed is published in full. The verification logic is open-source and reproducible offline. This is the same primitive that secured the very first Bitcoin casinos in 2014, refined and applied to 18 games.
