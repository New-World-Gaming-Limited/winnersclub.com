Kako deposit with Skrill at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter promo kod NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Skrill from the payment method list. Enter the deposit amount in USD, EUR, or GBP.
  3. You are redirected to a Skrill login page. Log in to your Skrill račun (or create one at skrill.com if you do not have one).
  4. Authorise the payment in Skrill. Confirm the amount and recipient (Stake). Skrill may send a one-time password (OTP) to your registered email or phone for 2FA confirmation.
  5. Return to Stake. The redirect brings you back to Stake automatically. Your wallet balance updates within 60 seconds. If it does not, check the Skrill transaction history and contact Stake live-chat with the Skrill transaction ID.

Kako withdraw from Stake to Skrill

  1. Complete KYC on Stake. Upload a photo ID before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Skrill.
  3. Enter your Skrill account email address. This is the email you use to log in to Skrill. Verify it carefully.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Confirm the Skrill email on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the withdrawal within 15 minutes to 3 hours. Once credited, funds appear instantly in your Skrill balance. You then withdraw from Skrill to your bank or card as needed.

Ograničenja i rokovi

Skrill račun limits depend on your verification level. Unverified Skrill računs have a cumulative lifetime upload limit of 2,500 EUR and cannot withdraw to a bank. Verified računs (passport/ID submitted) have higher limits: up to 900,000 EUR per year in uploads and withdrawals. For Stake deposits, the practical limit is whichever is lower: your Skrill račun limit or Stake's own maximum.

Skrill deposits at Stake are instant, making it one of the best e-wallet options for time-sensitive deposits before a match starts. Isplataals from Stake back to Skrill process within the hour for most requests during business hours.

Skrill charges a 4.99% fee for deposits funded by credit or debit card. If you fund your Skrill wallet via bank transfer first, you avoid this card surcharge. Funding via cryptocurrency to Skrill is also free of this fee.

Dostupnost prema zemlji i valuti

Skrill is available in 120+ countries and supports 40+ currencies. In Europe, USD, EUR, and GBP are the primary currencies. Skrill is not available in some countries including the United States (for gambling transactions), Nerth Korea, and a few others on the restricted list. If you are in a region where Skrill is restricted, use Neteller, crypto, or local mobile money at Stake instead.

FAQ: Skrill at Stake

What is the minimum Skrill deposit at Stake?

Minimum Skrill deposit at Stake is $10 USD (or equivalent in EUR or GBP). The exact minimum depends on your Skrill račun currency.

How fast are Skrill deposits at Stake?

Skrill deposits are instant. Your Stake wallet updates within 60 seconds of completing payment in the Skrill flow.

Can I withdraw from Stake to Skrill?

Da. Isplataals to Skrill are supported. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours from Stake's side; once credited to Skrill, funds are available instantly in your Skrill wallet.

Does Skrill charge fees for Stake transactions?
