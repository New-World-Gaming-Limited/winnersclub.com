Stake Launches LATAM Tournament Series with Ignacio Bahamondes

Tournaments By Conrad Castleton · 29 May 2026
  • Stake has launched a regional LATAM tournament series fronted by brand ambassador Ignacio Bahamondes.
  • Argentina, Chile and Brazil each have separate prize pools and winning places.
  • Players can compete for prizes across the June tournament schedule.

Stake has lined up a new LATAM tournament series fronted by its brand ambassador Ignacio Bahamondes, a Chilean UFC lightweight with a growing international profile.

Bahamondes brings a strong combat sports identity to the campaign. The Santiago-born striker is known for his attacking style, highlight-reel finishes and rise from the regional MMA scene to the UFC. Before joining the UFC, he became LUX Fight League lightweight champion, building the reputation that helped take him onto the international stage.

As a Stake ambassador, Bahamondes gives the LATAM tournament series a direct link to the region's combat sports audience, with separate events running in Argentina, Chile and Brazil.

What Is the Stake LATAM Tournament Series?

The Stake LATAM Tournament Series is a set of regional prize tournaments running in Argentina, Chile and Brazil.

Each country has its own tournament window, prize pool and number of winning places. Players join the tournament for their region and compete for a place in the final prize standings.

The series gives LATAM players a localised Stake tournament experience, with prizes listed in Argentine pesos, Chilean pesos and Brazilian reais.

Before joining the tournament, players should open the live tournament page and check the qualifying rules, scoring method and prize distribution for their country.
