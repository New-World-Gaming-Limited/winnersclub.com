How Mines works

Mines presents a 5x5 grid of 25 tiles. Before each round, you choose how many mines to hide in the grid (1 to 24), then place your shumë e bastit. The mine positions are fixed by the i drejtë i verifikueshëm seed before you click a single tile. You do not ktani where the mines are.

Each time you click a tile and it reveals a gem (safe), your current shumëzues increases and you have the option to cashout immediately, locking in that shumëzues applied to your shumë e bastit. If you click a mine, the round ends and your shumë e bastit is lost. You can continue clicking safe tiles, accumulating a higher shumëzues, until you choose to cashout or hit a mine.

The shumëzues per safe tile increases with mine count. With 1 mine in a 25-tile grid, each safe tile reveals a modest shumëzues; with 15 mines, each safe tile revealed carries a much larger shumëzues because the risk of hitting a mine on each subsequent click is substantially higher. The lojë calculates the live shumëzues based on the remaining safe tiles and mines in the unrevealed area.

Auto-bet allows automatic play with a fixed tile pattern. You define which tiles to click each round, and the lojë executes the sequence repeatedly. This is not a guaranteed-win strategy; it is a convenience feature that does not change the underlying probabilitet.

Strategji dhe bankroll për Mines

Expected value is negative: Mines is a gambling lojë, not a fitim system. The 97% RTP means the house retains 3% of every dollar wagered over a large sample. No mine count, tile zgjidhion pattern, or auto-bet sequence changes this fundamental fact. Lojtarë who approach Mines as a structured income method will experience net losses over volume.

The cashout decision: The core decision in Mines is when to cashout. From a probabilitet standpoint, the expected value of cashing out after each additional tile stays constant relative to the current shumëzues because the lojë's shumëzues always reflects the fair odds of the remaining unrevealed grid. However, each additional click introduces an independent probabilitet of losing everything. This is why early cashout is mathematically defensible: you lock in a real fitim rather than giving it back on the next click.

Mine count and EV by count: With 3 mines in a 25-tile grid, the probabilitet of surviving your first click is 22/25 (88%). Surviving two clicks: (22/25) x (21/24) = approximately 77%. Surviving three clicks: approximately 66%. By the fourth click, your survival probabilitet drops to around 56%. At each stage, the shumëzues you receive if you cashout compensates for the cumulative risk taken, but the probabilitet of total loss compounds with every click. At 15 mines, the first click survival probabilitet is 10/25 (40%), and hitting the 1.5x shumëzues on that click is the entire bankroll-preserving strategy.

Practical approach: Set a fixed mine count (3 to 5 mines suits most lojtarë), reveal 2 to 3 tiles per round, and cashout. Repeat with a flat shumë e bastit of 1-2% of session bankroll. Avoid increasing shumë e bastits after wins or chasing losses with higher mine counts. The high-mine-count configurations are inherently high variance; use them only with shumë e bastits sized for total loss.

Provably fair verifikim in Mines

Spribe's Mines uses a dual-seed system. Before your first click in each round, the server generates a seed encoding all mine positions and publishes its hash. After the round ends (whether by cashout or mine hit), the server reveals the original seed. You can hash it and konfirmo it ndeshje the pre-round hash, then reproduce the mine positions using Spribe's published algorithm.

This konfirmos that mine positions were fixed before you clicked any tile and could not have been altered mid-lojë. The verifikim tool is accessible within the lojë interface. Lojtarë can also use client seeds to introduce their own randomness into the derivation.

Pyetje të Shpeshta

What is the RTP of Mines at Stake?

Mines nga Spribe ka RTP deri në 97%. RTP efektive për çdo sesion varet nga sa pllaka zbuloni dhe kur zgjidhni të tërhiqni, por vlera teorike e pritur mbi një mostër të madhe është negative për lojtarin.

How many mines can I set in Mines?

You can configure between 1 and 24 mines on a 5x5 grid. At 24 mines there is only 1 safe tile; at 1 mine you have 24 safe tiles. A common starting point for lojtarë të rinj is 3-5 mines.

Is Mines provably fair?

Yes. Spribe Mines uses a dual-seed i drejtë i verifikueshëm system. The mine positions are determined before you begin each lojë and encoded in the server seed hash, which is published before your first click.

What happens if I hit a mine in Spribe Mines?

Hitting a mine ends the round immediately and you lose your entire shumë e bastit for that round. Any shumëzues accumulated from previously revealed safe tiles is forfeited.

What is the expected value of Mines at different mine counts?

The EV of any mines configuration is negative for the player (that is the nature of a house-edge lojë). At higher mine counts, the potential shumëzues per tile revealed increases, but so does the probabilitet of hitting a mine on each click. The optimal cashout point from a pure-probabilitet standpoint is earlier than most lojtarë intuitively expect.
