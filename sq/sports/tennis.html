Most i popullarizuar tenis betting tregje

  • Match winner - Back either player to win the ndeshje. The foundation treg, i disponueshëm from qualifying to finals on every tour ngjarje.
  • Set winner - Bet on which player wins a specific set (Set 1, Set 2, etc.) rather than the overall ndeshje. Useful in-play as momentum shifts after a tight set.
  • Set betting (correct set score) - Predict the exact scoreline in sets: 2-0, 2-1 for best-of-three; 3-0, 3-1, 3-2 for Grand Slams. Enhanced odds reward precise forecasting.
  • Total lojëra (over/under) - Will the total number of lojëra played exceed or fall below a stated line? A competitive ndeshje between Djokovic and Alcaraz often goes deep into a deciding set, pushing totals above 40 lojëra.
  • Total sets - Will the ndeshje be decided in 2 sets or go to 3 (best-of-three), or reach 4 or 5 in a Grand Slam? Pre-ndeshje form data on sets won drives this treg.
  • Handicap (lojë handicap) - One player is given a lojë advantage or disadvantage. If Djokovic is -4.5 lojëra, he needs to win by 5 or more net lojëra across the ndeshje.
  • To win first set and ndeshje - A double-result combination i popullarizuar with akumulators. Taking the more likely set winner and ndeshje winner at combined odds adds value versus two separate tregje.
