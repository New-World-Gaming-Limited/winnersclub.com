가장 인기 있는 테니스 베팅 마켓

  • 경기 승자 - 두 선수 중 하나가 경기를 이기는 것에 베팅하세요. 기본 마켓으로, 모든 투어 이벤트의 예선부터 결승까지 이용 가능합니다.
  • Set winner - 전체 경기가 아닌 특정 세트(1세트, 2세트 등)를 이기는 선수에 베팅하세요. 치열한 세트 후 모멘텀이 변할 때 인플레이에서 유용합니다.
  • 세트 베팅 (정확한 세트 점수) - 세트의 정확한 점수를 예측하세요: 3판 2선승의 경우 2-0, 2-1; 그랜드 슬램의 경우 3-0, 3-1, 3-2. 강화된 배당률이 정확한 예측에 보상합니다.
  • 총 게임 수 (오버/언더) - Will the total number of games played exceed or fall below a stated line? A competitive match between Djokovic and Alcaraz often goes deep into a deciding set, pushing totals above 40 games.
  • Total sets - Will the match be decided in 2 sets or go to 3 (best-of-three), or reach 4 or 5 in a Grand Slam? Pre-match form data on sets won drives this market.
  • 핸디캡 (게임 핸디캡) - One player is given a game advantage or disadvantage. If Djokovic is -4.5 games, he needs to win by 5 or more net games across the match.
  • 1세트 및 경기 승리 - A double-result combination popular with accumulators. Taking the more likely set winner and match winner at combined odds adds value versus two separate markets.

인플레이 및 라이브 테니스 베팅

Tennis is one of the best sports for live betting because the score resets at the start of every game, creating constant odds movement. Stake's in-play tennis interface updates within seconds of each point, letting you react to a break of serve, a mid-match injury timeout or a sudden shift in momentum that is visible before the bookmaker recalibrates fully.

Pre-match research is essential: surface win rate, head-to-head record, recent form on the tour and serving statistics (first serve percentage, aces per match, double faults) all inform a well-placed pre-match selection. In-play betting, however, is where tennis delivers its greatest edge. When Djokovic loses the first set at Wimbledon, live odds on his recovery often over-correct relative to his career comeback rate, presenting value for the in-play bettor who knows his historical second-set records.

Key in-play markets at Stake include: next game winner, current set winner, next set score, number of aces in the next game and whether the current game will go to deuce. Select ATP and WTA matches are live-streamed on the platform.
