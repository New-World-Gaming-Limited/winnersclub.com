What is the RTP of Plinko at Stake?

Plinko by Spribe has an RTP of up to 97%, which applies at the low-risk setting. Higher risk levels maintain the same theoretical RTP but redistribute payouts toward rarer, larger wins.

What does the risk level setting do in Plinko?

The risk level (low, medium, high) controls how payouts are distributed across the multiplier slots at the bottom. Low risk gives frequent small wins with a low maximum multiplier. High risk gives a higher maximum multiplier but far fewer winning slots; most drops land in low or zero payout positions.

How many rows can I choose in Plinko?

Plinko allows 8 to 16 rows. More rows mean more peg interactions and a more spread-out multiplier distribution. The central slots at the bottom remain the most likely landing zone regardless of row count.
