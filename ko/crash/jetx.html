The two fundamental approaches in JetX are low-multiplier disciplined play and high-multiplier hunting. Understanding the mathematics of each is important before committing real money.

At a 97% RTP, a theoretical 2x game would require the bet to succeed roughly 48.5% of the time across a large sample. In reality, rounds crash below 2x frequently enough that disciplined flat betting at 2x produces slow bankroll erosion over volume. The advantage is that losing streaks are shorter and each win is defined. Use a unit stake of no more than 1-2% of your session bankroll.
