NEFT innskudd at Stake for Indian spillere

NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer) is a batch-based bank transfer method accepted at Stake for INR innskudds. The plattform holds a Curaçao 1668/JAZ licence and is trusted by 400,000+ spillere worldwide. NEFT has no per-transaction cap set by RBI, making it suitable for large innskudds that exceed UPI or IMPS limits. How to innskudd using NEFT at Stake

  1. Log in and open the Cashier. Select "Deposit," then choose NEFT from the betalingsmetode list.
  2. Copy the Stake beneficiary details. The cashier displays the konto number, IFSC code, and konto name. Screenshot this screen.
  3. Log in to your bank's net banking or mobil app. Add Stake as a NEFT beneficiary using the details from step 2. Beneficiary addition may require a 30-minute bank cool-down on forste-time additions.
  4. Initiate the NEFT transfer. Select NEFT, choose the Stake beneficiary, enter the amount (minimum 500 INR), and include your Stake user ID in the payment remarks field. Confirm with your net banking passord or OTP.
  5. Wait for the batch to settle. NEFT batches run every 30 minutes. You will get a UTR confirmation SMS from your bank. Your Stake wallet updates after the batch settles, typically within 30 to 120 minutes. Keep the UTR in case you need to follow up with support.

How to withdraw via NEFT from Stake

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Upload PAN card or Aadhaar and a bank proof document before your forste uttak.
  2. Open Cashier and click Withdraw. Select NEFT.
  3. Enter your bank konto number, IFSC code, and konto holder name. These must match your bank records exactly.
  4. Enter the uttak amount. NEFT uttaks have no system-level cap, but Stake's risk team may request additional verification for very large uttaks.
  5. Submit. Stake queues your uttak request. NEFT credits arrive in 2 to 12 hours depending on when the next batch settles at your bank. Your bank sends an SMS on credit.

Grenser og tidsfrister

RBI removed the NEFT per-transaction ceiling in December 2019, meaning there is no cap on individual NEFT transfers at the network level. However, your bank may still impose daily transfer limits through net banking (common caps are 5 lakh to 25 lakh per day depending on the bank and konto type). Check your bank's net banking settings under "Transaction Limits" to see your specific cap.

NEFT operates 24/7 on all days of the year including Sundays and public holidays since December 2019. Batches settle every 30 minutes, starting at midnight. If you initiate a transfer at 2:15 PM, the batch settles at 2:30 PM and Stake should receive the funds by 3:00 PM at the latest.

NEFT er tregere enn IMPS eller UPI for små beløp, men for overføringer over 5 lakh er det et pålitelig alternativ uten IMPS sine sporadiske flagginger for høye verdier.

Tilgjengelighet per land og valuta

NEFT is tilgjengelig at all banks that are RBI members in India, covering virtually all public and private sector banks, regional rural banks, and cooperative banks. NEFT transfers are in INR only. Stake's receiving bank konto is an Indian bank konto denominated in INR.

Non-resident Indians (NRI) with NRE or NRO kontos can also use NEFT if their bank allows outward domestic transfers from these kontos.

Alternative betalingsmetodes

  • IMPS - faster settlement, up to 5 lakh per transaction
  • UPI - instant, app-based, 1 lakh cap
  • Net banking - integrated bank login flow
  • Bitcoin - crypto for large transfers without bank-level limits

FAQ: NEFT at Stake

How long does a NEFT innskudd take at Stake?

NEFT processes in half-hourly batches 24/7. Deposits typically credit to your Stake wallet within 30 to 120 minutes after the bank initiates the transfer.

Is there a maksimum limit on NEFT innskudds at Stake?

RBI har fjernet NEFT-transaksjons­taket. Teoretisk sett kan du overføre ethvert beløp. I praksis kan banken din pålegge daglige eller per-transaksjons­grenser; sjekk nettbankinnstillingene dine.

What is the minimum NEFT innskudd at Stake?

Minimum NEFT innskudd is 500 INR.

Does Stake charge a fee for NEFT innskudds?

Stake charges no fee. Your bank may charge up to 25 INR for outward NEFT transfers depending pa ditt konto type.

My NEFT innskudd has not arrived after 2 hours. What should I do?

Collect your bank's NEFT UTR number and the transfer acknowledgement. Contact Stake live-chat and provide both the UTR and your Stake user ID. The payments team can locate and manually post the credit.
