How to innskudd with Neteller at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Registrer deg forste if ny; enter kampanjekode MAXBET.
  2. Select Neteller from the payment options. Enter the innskudd amount in USD or EUR.
  3. Log in to Neteller. You are redirected to a Neteller-hosted page. Enter your Neteller konto email and secure ID.
  4. Approve the payment. Confirm the merchant name (Stake) and amount. Complete any 2FA required by Neteller (email code or authenticator app).
  5. Return to Stake. The browser returns to Stake automatisk. Your wallet balance updates within 60 andres. Check Neteller transaction history for the Neteller Transaction ID if you need to follow up.

How to withdraw from Stake to Neteller

  1. Complete Stake identity verification. Submit a government-issued photo ID before your forste uttak.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Neteller.
  3. Enter your Neteller konto email and the uttak amount. The email must match the Neteller konto you want to receive funds in.
  4. Confirm on the summary screen. Double-check the email address.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the uttak in 15 minutes to 3 hours. Funds appear in your Neteller wallet balance umiddelbart upon receipt. From there, transfer to your bank or use the Net+ card.

Grenser og tidsfrister

Neteller's monthly upload limit for unverified kontos is 2,500 EUR. Verified kontos (with submitted ID and proof of address) have higher limits, potentially up to 1,000,000 EUR annually. For Stake uttaks to Neteller, the effective limit is whichever is smaller: Stake's configured uttak maksimum or your Neteller konto's tilgjengelig balance capacity.

Neteller processes transfers using the Paysafe Group infrastructure, which operates 24/7. Deposits are instant. Withdrawal timing from Stake to Neteller is determined by Stake's payment queue, not Neteller's processing speed. Once Stake dispatches the transfer, Neteller credits it umiddelbart.

Neteller's Net+ prepaid Mastercard allows spending of Neteller balance at any Mastercard-accepting merchant worldwide. This gives you a direct path from Stake winnings to spendable funds: Stake uttak to Neteller (instant once dispatched), then spend via Net+ card.
