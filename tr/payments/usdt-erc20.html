How to deposit USDT ERC20 at Stake

  1. Stake'e giriş yap ve Kasa'yı aç. Yeni kullanıcılar: kayıt ol ve promosyon kodunu gir NEWBONUS.
  2. Select USDT ERC20 (USDT) from the cryptocurrency deposit options. Ensure you select the correct network: Ethereum (ERC20). Sending USDT on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Stake USDT para yatırma adresini kopyala. Sana özel bir cüzdan adresi oluşturulur. Bu adresi başkalarıyla paylaşma. Ek güvenlik için adresi elle yazmak yerine QR kodu tara.
  4. Kripto cüzdanından veya borsandan USDT gönder. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake deposit address, enter the amount (minimum 10 USDT), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Blockchain onaylarını bekle. The Ethereum (ERC20) network requires 12 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. 3 to 5 minutes (12 Ethereum confirmations).

How to withdraw USDT ERC20 from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto withdrawals.
  2. Kasa'yı aç ve Para Çekme'yi seç. Choose USDT ERC20 (USDT).
  3. USDT cüzdan adresini gir. This must be an address on the Ethereum (ERC20) network. Double-check the address character by character; crypto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Para çekme miktarını gir. Stake, zincir üstü işlem maliyetlerini karşılamak için tutardan bir ağ ücreti keser. Ücret onaylamadan önce gösterilir.
  5. İşlemi gönder ve takip et. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the Ethereum (ERC20) network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Para Çekme processing takes 15 to 60 minutes after submission.

Limitler ve zamanlama for USDT ERC20 deposits and withdrawals

USDT on the Ethereum (ERC20) network has the broadest wallet and exchange support of any USDT variant, but at a higher cost. Ethereum block time is approximately 12 seconds, and Stake requires 12 confirmations, placing typical deposit confirmation time at 3 to 5 minutes under normal conditions.

Ethereum gas fees vary significantly with network load. In quiet periods, a USDT ERC20 transfer costs $2 to $10 USD in gas. During DeFi activity spikes, fees can reach $50 to $100+ USD per transaction. For deposits under $100 USD, USDT TRC20 is almost always cheaper. USDT ERC20 is worth using if your source wallet or exchange does not support TRC20 withdrawal.

There is no Stake maximum deposit cap for USDT ERC20. Para Çekmes process within 15 to 60 minutes of request submission.
