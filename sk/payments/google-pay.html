Ako deposit using Google Pay at Stake

  1. Log in to your Stake account. New users: register first and enter promo kód NEWBONUS.
  2. Click Deposit in the cashier. The payment method panel opens. Select Google Pay or the generic UPI option.
  3. Enter the deposit amount. Minimum is 300 INR. The screen will display a QR code or UPI payment link.
  4. Open Google Pay on your phone. Tap "Scan QR code," point at the screen, or tap the UPI deep-link on mobile to jump directly into GPay. Confirm the amount and approve with your UPI PIN.
  5. Check your Stake wallet. Funds appear within 60 seconds. If not, note the GPay transaction ID (visible in GPay under transaction history) and contact Stake live-chat support.

Ako withdraw to Google Pay from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Submit a valid government-issued photo ID in your Stake profile settings before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Withdrawal section of the cashier.
  3. Select Google Pay (UPI). Enter your GPay VPA. Standard formats: mobilenumber@okaxis, mobilenumber@okhdfcbank, mobilenumber@okicici, mobilenumber@oksbi.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Double-check the VPA. An error in the VPA will cause the funds to reach the wrong účet with no recourse.
  5. Submit. The wallet deducts immediately. Funds arrive in 15 minutes to 3 hours; you will get a GPay notification on credit.

Limity a lehoty

Google Pay on UPI inherits the NPCI per-transaction limit of 1,00,000 INR. Google also sets its own daily aggregate limit, which for fully KYC-verified účets is 1,00,000 INR per day. Some banks that participate in GPay set lower limits; check your bank's UPI limit in your net banking portal if you experience declines at high amounts.

For deposits, timing is effectively instant: the NPCI switch confirms transactions in real time. For withdrawals, Stake's payment processor queues and dispatches the request, which typically completes within the first hour during the working day. Night-time withdrawals (midnight to 6 AM IST) may take slightly longer as the banking batch settlement runs.

Nie cap on the number of daily GPay deposits from Stake's side, but if your bank declines after three failed attempts in a row, UPI may temporarily block further attempts to that VPA for up to 24 hours. In that case, use a different UPI app or crypto.

Dostupnosť podľa krajiny a mena

Google Pay UPI is available in India only. Stake processes GPay transactions exclusively in INR. If you have an overseas Google Pay účet, it operates on different payment rails and is not the same product; Stake's UPI integration applies only to Indian účets.

Google Pay is available on Android and iOS. The app requires an Indian mobile number and an Indian bank účet to create a UPI VPA. As of 2024, Google Pay is the second-largest UPI app in India by transaction volume, ensuring high reliability and uptime.

FAQ: Google Pay at Stake

Can I use Google Pay for Stake deposits in India?

Áno. Google Pay (GPay) uses the UPI rail and is fully supported at Stake for INR deposits.

What Google Pay VPA format does Stake accept?

Stake accepts any valid UPI VPA including the standard Google Pay formats: mobilenumber@okaxis, mobilenumber@okhdfcbank, mobilenumber@okicici, mobilenumber@oksbi.

Is there a Google Pay withdrawal option at Stake?

Áno. Enter your GPay VPA in the withdrawal form. Funds land in the bank účet linked to that VPA within 15 minutes to 3 hours.

My GPay payment was declined. Why?

Common reasons: daily UPI limit reached on your bank účet, incorrect VPA, or your bank temporarily flagging the transaction. Try a smaller amount or switch to a different UPI app on the same účet.

Does Stake charge any fee for Google Pay transactions?

Nie. Stake does not charge any fee. Google Pay itself does not charge for UPI P2P transfers in India.
