Ako deposit at Stake with Orange Money

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register with promo kód NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Orange Money from the payment list. Enter the deposit amount in your local currency.
  3. Note the Stake Orange Money merchant number. This is displayed on the cashier screen.
  4. Open the Orange Money app or dial the USSD code for your country (e.g., *144# in Senegal, *144# in Ivory Coast). Select "Merchant Payment" or "Payer un marchand." Enter the Stake merchant number, the amount, and your Stake user ID as the reference. Confirm with your Orange Money secret code.
  5. Wait for SMS confirmation. Your Stake balance updates within 5 minutes. Keep the Orange Money transaction reference number.

Ako withdraw from Stake to Orange Money

  1. Complete identity verification on Stake. Submit a valid national ID before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Orange Money.
  3. Enter your Orange Money registered phone number. Format varies by country: Senegal 77/78-XXXXXXX, Ivory Coast 07/08-XXXXXXX.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount and confirm. Verify that the name on your Orange Money účet matches your Stake registration.
  5. Submit. Funds arrive in 15 minutes to 2 hours. An Orange Money SMS confirms the credit.

Limity a lehoty

Orange Money limits vary by country and účet level. In Senegal, standard účets allow XOF 200,000 per day; in Ivory Coast, XOF 300,000 per day for registered účets. Advanced účets (full KYC with biometric verification) have higher limits, up to XOF 2,000,000 per month in many markets.

Orange Money operates 24/7 but may have scheduled maintenance windows between 2 AM and 4 AM local time. During maintenance, deposits may be delayed by up to 15 minutes. The system automatically retries and settles pending transactions after maintenance ends.

Orange Money is particularly popular in Francophone West Africa, where it competes directly with MTN MoMo. In countries where both are available, both are typically accepted at Stake.
