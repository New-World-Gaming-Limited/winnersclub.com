Ako deposit Litecoin at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter promo kód MAXBET.
  2. Select Litecoin (LTC) from the cryptocurrency deposit options. Ensure you select the correct network: Litecoin. Sending LTC on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake LTC deposit address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send LTC from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake deposit address, enter the amount (minimum 0.05 LTC), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.

Ako withdraw Litecoin from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto withdrawals.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Litecoin (LTC).

Limity a lehoty for Litecoin deposits and withdrawals

Litecoin produces a new block every 2.5 minutes (4x faster than Bitcoin). Stake requires 6 Litecoin confirmations, so deposits typically credit in 15 to 20 minutes. This is significantly faster than Bitcoin's average 30-40 minute deposit time at 3 confirmations.

Litecoin transaction fees are very low compared to Bitcoin and Ethereum. A standard LTC transfer typically costs 0.001 LTC or less in network fees (well under $0.10 USD at current prices). This makes LTC attractive for smaller, frequent deposits where Bitcoin fees would eat into the transferred amount.
