$1M Bad Beat Jackpot

Active at all Hold'em cash tables. The nastier the beat, the bigger the payout.

The Bad Beat Jackpot triggers when you lose holding quads or better, 2222 in Hold'em, 4444 in PLO4, 8888 in PLO5, JJJJ in PLO6, using both hole cards, with at least 4 players dealt in to the hand. The pot can reach up to $1,000,000. Payout split: losing player receives 5.6% of the pool, winning player gets 3.5%, and all other active players share 2.1%. Three percent of every raked pot feeds this pool, so it grows continuously across all Hold'em tables. A second jackpot operates at All-in or Fold tables separately.
