How to ฝากเงิน Litecoin at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter รหัสโปรโมชั่น MAXBET.
  2. Select Litecoin (LTC) from the cryptocurrency ฝากเงิน options. Ensure you select the correct network: Litecoin. Sending LTC on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake LTC ฝากเงิน address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send LTC from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake ฝากเงิน address, enter the amount (minimum 0.05 LTC), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.

How to withdraw Litecoin from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto ถอนเงินs.
  2. Open the Cashier and select ถอนเงิน. Choose Litecoin (LTC).

วงเงินและเวลา for Litecoin ฝากเงินs and ถอนเงินs

Litecoin produces a new block every 2.5 minutes (4x faster than Bitcoin). Stake requires 6 Litecoin confirmations, so ฝากเงินs typically credit in 15 to 20 minutes. This is significantly faster than Bitcoin's average 30-40 minute ฝากเงิน time at 3 confirmations.

Litecoin transaction fees are very low compared to Bitcoin and Ethereum. A standard LTC transfer typically costs 0.001 LTC or less in network fees (well under $0.10 USD at current prices). This makes LTC attractive for smaller, frequent ฝากเงินs where Bitcoin fees would eat into the transferred amount.

Minimum LTC ฝากเงิน at Stake is 0.05 LTC. There is no maximum cap from Stake's side. ถอนเงิน fees are deducted from the sent amount; shown before you confirm.
