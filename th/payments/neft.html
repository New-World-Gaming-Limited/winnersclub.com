NEFT ฝากเงิน at Stake for Indian players

NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer) is a batch-based bank transfer method accepted at Stake for INR ฝากเงินs. The platform holds a Curaçao 1668/JAZ licence and is trusted by 400,000+ players worldwide. NEFT has no per-transaction cap set by RBI, making it suitable for large ฝากเงินs that exceed UPI or IMPS limits. สมัครสมาชิก with รหัสโปรโมชั่น MAXBET to activate the โบนัสต้อนรับ on your first ฝากเงิน.

How to ฝากเงิน using NEFT at Stake

  1. Log in and open the Cashier. Select "ฝากเงิน," then choose NEFT from the การชำระเงิน method list.
  2. Copy the Stake beneficiary details. The cashier displays the account number, IFSC code, and account name. Screenshot this screen.
  3. Log in to your bank's net banking or แอปมือถือ. Add Stake as a NEFT beneficiary using the details from step 2. Beneficiary addition may require a 30-minute bank cool-down on first-time additions.
  4. Initiate the NEFT transfer. Select NEFT, choose the Stake beneficiary, enter the amount (minimum 500 INR), and include your Stake user ID in the การชำระเงิน remarks field. Confirm with your net banking password or OTP.
  5. Wait for the batch to settle. NEFT batches run every 30 minutes. You will get a UTR confirmation SMS from your bank. Your Stake wallet updates after the batch settles, typically within 30 to 120 minutes. Keep the UTR in case you need to follow up with support.

How to withdraw via NEFT from Stake

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Upload PAN card or Aadhaar and a bank proof document before your first ถอนเงิน.
  2. Open Cashier and click Withdraw. Select NEFT.
  3. Enter your bank account number, IFSC code, and account holder name. These must match your bank records exactly.
  4. Enter the ถอนเงิน amount. NEFT ถอนเงินs have no system-level cap, but Stake's risk team may request additional verification for very large ถอนเงินs.
  5. Submit. Stake queues your ถอนเงิน request. NEFT credits arrive in 2 to 12 hours depending on when the next batch settles at your bank. Your bank sends an SMS on credit.

วงเงินและเวลา

RBI removed the NEFT per-transaction ceiling in December 2019, meaning there is no cap on individual NEFT transfers at the network level. However, your bank may still impose daily transfer limits through net banking (common caps are 5 lakh to 25 lakh per day depending on the bank and account type). Check your bank's net banking settings under "Transaction Limits" to see your specific cap.

NEFT operates 24/7 on all days of the year including Sundays and public holidays since December 2019. Batches settle every 30 minutes, starting at midnight. If you initiate a transfer at 2:15 PM, the batch settles at 2:30 PM and Stake should receive the funds by 3:00 PM at the latest.

NEFT ช้ากว่า IMPS หรือ UPI สำหรับจำนวนเงินน้อย แต่สำหรับการโอนเกิน 5 แสน ยังคงเป็นตัวเลือกที่น่าเชื่อถือโดยไม่มีการตั้งค่าสถานะมูลค่าสูงของ IMPS บางครั้ง

ประเทศที่รองรับและสกุลเงิน

NEFT is available at all banks that are RBI members in India, covering virtually all public and private sector banks, regional rural banks, and cooperative banks. NEFT transfers are in INR only. Stake's receiving bank account is an Indian bank account denominated in INR.

Non-resident Indians (NRI) with NRE or NRO accounts can also use NEFT if their bank allows outward domestic transfers from these accounts.

Alternative การชำระเงิน methods

  • IMPS - faster settlement, up to 5 lakh per transaction
  • UPI - instant, app-based, 1 lakh cap
  • Net banking - integrated bank login flow
  • Bitcoin - crypto for large transfers without bank-level limits
  • USDT TRC20 - stablecoin with fast settlement

คำถามที่พบบ่อย: NEFT at Stake

How long does a NEFT ฝากเงิน take at Stake?

NEFT processes in half-hourly batches 24/7. ฝากเงินs typically credit to your Stake wallet within 30 to 120 minutes after the bank initiates the transfer.

Is there a maximum limit on NEFT ฝากเงินs at Stake?

RBI ได้ยกเลิกข้อจำกัดธุรกรรม NEFT ในทางทฤษฎีคุณสามารถโอนเงินจำนวนใดก็ได้ ในทางปฏิบัติธนาคารของคุณอาจกำหนดขีดจำกัดรายวันหรือต่อธุรกรรม ตรวจสอบการตั้งค่าธนาคารออนไลน์ของคุณ

What is the minimum NEFT ฝากเงิน at Stake?

Minimum NEFT ฝากเงิน is 500 INR.

Does Stake charge a fee for NEFT ฝากเงินs?

Stake charges no fee. Your bank may charge up to 25 INR for outward NEFT transfers depending on your account type.

My NEFT ฝากเงิน has not arrived after 2 hours. What should I do?

Collect your bank's NEFT UTR number and the transfer acknowledgement. Contact Stake live-chat and provide both the UTR and your Stake user ID. The การชำระเงินs team can locate and manually post the credit.
