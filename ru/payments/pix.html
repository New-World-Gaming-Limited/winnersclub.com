Deposito via PIX na Stake: como fazer (Как пополнить через PIX at Stake)

  1. Crie sua conta / Register your Stake account. New users: complete the registration form and enter promo code NEWBONUS to activate the welcome bonus.
  2. Acesse o Caixa (Open the Cashier). Click the green "Deposit" button. The cashier panel opens.
  3. Selecione PIX (Select PIX). Choose PIX from the payment method list. Enter the BRL amount you want to deposit (minimum R$20).
  4. Escaneie o QR code (Scan the QR code). A PIX QR code and a copy-paste PIX key string appear on screen. Open your bank's app (Itau, Bradesco, Nubank, Caixa, BTG, or any PIX-enabled bank), navigate to "PIX," tap "Pagar via QR code," and scan the code. Alternatively, paste the PIX copy-paste key directly.
  5. Confirme o pagamento (Confirm). Your Stake balance updates in under 60 seconds. A confirmation email arrives shortly after. Keep the PIX end-to-end transaction ID (E2EID) from your bank app in case you need to contact support.

How to withdraw from Stake via PIX

  1. Complete account verification. Submit CPF and a valid Brazilian ID (RG or CNH) in your Stake profile before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose PIX.
  3. Enter your PIX chave (key). This is typically your CPF number, registered mobile number, or email address linked to your bank account. PIX keys are case-sensitive when using email format.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Check that the name on the PIX key matches your Stake-registered name; mismatches can delay or reject the transfer.
  5. Submit. Stake processes withdrawals within 30 to 60 minutes during business hours. PIX transfers complete in seconds on the banking side, so the total time is mostly queue-dependent on Stake's end.

Лимиты и сроки

Banco Central do Brasil (BCB) устанавливает лимиты PIX на уровне отдельного банка. Для физических лиц дневной лимит по умолчанию — R$20 000 BRL за транзакцию, ночной лимит (с 20:00 до 06:00) ниже, обычно R$1 000 BRL. Вы можете запросить повышение лимита через приложение вашего банка, как правило, вступающее в силу в течение 24 часов.

PIX is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Unlike TED (which requires bank business hours) or boleto (which requires manual processing), PIX has no blackout windows. This makes it ideal for late-night deposits after a match or event you want to bet on.

CPF validation: PIX keys linked to a CPF are validated by the DICT (Diretorio de Identificadores de Contas Transacionais) system. If the CPF on the PIX key does not match the name registered on your Stake account, the withdrawal may be returned. Ensure your Stake registration name exactly matches your bank account name.

Доступность по странам и валюты

PIX is available in Brazil only, for individuals and businesses registered with Brazilian financial institutions. All transactions are in BRL. Stake's PIX integration processes payments in real time through a Brazilian payment aggregator regulated by BCB.

Brazilian residents can create a PIX key using any of four key types: CPF/CNPJ, mobile phone number, email address, or an EVP (Evolutionary Virtual Key) generated by their bank. Any of these key types are accepted at Stake for withdrawals.

Can I withdraw from Stake to my PIX key?
