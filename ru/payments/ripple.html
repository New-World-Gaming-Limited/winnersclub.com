How to deposit Ripple at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter promo code MAXBET.
  2. Select Ripple (XRP) from the cryptocurrency deposit options. Ensure you select the correct network: XRP Ledger. Sending XRP on the wrong network results in permanent loss of funds.
  3. Copy the Stake XRP deposit address. A unique wallet address is generated for you. Do not share this address with others. For added safety, scan the QR code instead of manually typing the address.
  4. Send XRP from your crypto wallet or exchange. Open your wallet (Binance, Coinbase, Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.), paste the Stake deposit address, enter the amount (minimum 5 XRP), and confirm the transaction. Double-check the address and network before confirming.
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmations. The XRP Ledger network requires 6 confirmations. Track the transaction on a blockchain explorer using the TXID. Once confirmed, Stake credits your wallet automatically. Under 30 seconds (6 XRP Ledger confirmations).

How to withdraw Ripple from Stake

  1. Complete KYC if required. Stake may require identity verification before large crypto withdrawals.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Ripple (XRP).
  3. Enter your XRP wallet address. This must be an address on the XRP Ledger network. Double-check the address character by character; crypto transfers are irreversible.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Stake deducts a network fee from the amount to cover on-chain transaction costs. The fee is shown before you confirm.
  5. Submit and track the transaction. Stake broadcasts the transaction to the XRP Ledger network. You receive the TXID to track on a blockchain explorer. Withdrawal processing takes 10 to 30 minutes after submission.

Лимиты и сроки for Ripple (XRP) deposits and withdrawals

XRP transactions settle in 3 to 5 seconds on the XRP Ledger. Stake requires 6 confirmations, meaning deposits typically credit in under 30 seconds. XRP is one of the fastest confirmation chains at Stake, comparable only to Solana and Tron.

XRP network fees are set by the protocol: a standard XRP transaction costs 0.00001 XRP (10 drops), which is a negligible fraction of a cent at any realistic XRP price. This makes XRP extremely cost-efficient for any deposit size.

Important note: When depositing XRP at Stake, a destination tag (memo) is required. The cashier displays a unique destination tag when you generate your deposit address. Sending XRP without the correct destination tag may result in your funds being unallocated. Contact live-chat with your TXID and destination tag if you forget to include it.

FAQ: Ripple at Stake

What is the minimum Ripple deposit at Stake?

Minimum Ripple deposit at Stake is 5 XRP (approximately ~$3 USD at current rates). The minimum changes if XRP price moves significantly; always check the cashier for the current minimum.

How long does a Ripple deposit take to confirm?

Under 30 seconds (6 XRP Ledger confirmations). Ripple deposits require 6 network confirmations before crediting to your Stake wallet.

Does Stake charge a fee for Ripple deposits or withdrawals?

Stake does not charge a deposit fee. For withdrawals, Stake deducts a network fee from the withdrawal amount to cover the blockchain transaction cost. XRP Ledger fees are a tiny fraction of a cent per transaction (0.00001 XRP).

Can I use Ripple to avoid geographic restrictions on local banking?

Yes. XRP deposits and withdrawals are geographic-agnostic. Players in India, Pakistan, Africa, and other regions where local banking options are limited can use XRP without RBI, SBP, or local bank restrictions.

My Ripple deposit has not arrived. What should I do?

Сначала подтвердите, что транзакция имеет 6 подтверждений в проводнике блокчейна XRP Ledger по хешу транзакции (TXID). Если подтверждена, обратитесь в живой чат Stake с TXID. Если ещё не подтверждена, подождите, пока сеть её обработает.
