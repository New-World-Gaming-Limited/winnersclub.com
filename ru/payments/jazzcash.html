Как пополнить через JazzCash at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and click Deposit. New users: register first and enter promo code MAXBET.
  2. Select JazzCash from the cashier. Enter the PKR deposit amount (minimum PKR 500). The screen shows a Stake JazzCash mobile account number.
  3. Open the JazzCash app on your phone. Tap "Send Money" or "Mobile Account." Enter the Stake account number as the recipient.
  4. Confirm the amount and add your Stake user ID in the remarks field. Enter your JazzCash MPIN to authorise the transaction.
  5. Check your Stake balance. Funds appear within 5 minutes. Note the JazzCash transaction ID from the app confirmation screen.

How to withdraw from Stake to JazzCash

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Submit CNIC details and a selfie in the Profile verification section before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose JazzCash.
  3. Enter your JazzCash registered number. Format: 03XX-XXXXXXX. This must be a number with an active JazzCash mobile account.
  4. Enter withdrawal amount and confirm details. The recipient name shown should match your Stake registration name.
  5. Submit. Stake debits your wallet and dispatches the transfer. JazzCash credits your mobile account within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS confirms receipt.

Лимиты и сроки

JazzCash mobile account limits depend on your KYC level. Level 0 (unverified, registered with mobile number only): maximum wallet balance PKR 25,000, daily transactions PKR 25,000. Level 1 (CNIC verified): maximum wallet balance PKR 200,000, daily transactions PKR 200,000. Level 2 (biometric verification at Jazz franchise): PKR 500,000 per month limit.

To maximise your deposit and withdrawal limits at Stake via JazzCash, complete Level 1 verification by visiting any Jazz franchise with your CNIC. The process takes about 10 minutes and limits increase immediately after biometric registration.

JazzCash has 99%+ uptime and operates 24/7. Deposits are real-time confirmed. Withdrawals from Stake depend on the payment queue; most process within the first hour during business hours (9 AM to 10 PM PKT).

Доступность по странам и валюты

JazzCash is available to Pakistani residents with a Jazz or Warid SIM. As of 2024, JazzCash has over 25 million registered accounts and 130,000+ franchise points across Pakistan. All transactions are in PKR. JazzCash is regulated by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) as a Branchless Banking licence holder.

Non-Jazz SIM holders can also register for JazzCash using any Pakistani mobile number, though the wallet is primarily linked to Jazz/Warid numbers. Check JazzCash's website for non-Jazz registration options.
