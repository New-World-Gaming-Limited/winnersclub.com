How these football tips are generated

Each morning our automated pipeline scans the day's fixture list and identifies the 3 to 5 matches with the highest expected lažybos volume based on league tier, team global ranking, and rinka liquidity at Stake. These candidates are then apžvalgaed against four analytical layers: current form over the last 6 competitive fixtures (weighted by recency), head-to-head history on the specific ground or neutral venue, confirmed squad availability from official club injury reports, and Asian handicap line movement over the previous 12 hours.

Tips are not published if the closing-line probability implied by our model is within 3 percentage points of the bookmaker's published koeficientai. This margin threshold ensures that published selections reflect a meaningful expected edge rather than noise. The freshness window for tips is 24 hours. Tips from the previous day are archived after 23:59 UTC and naujas selections replace them at programėlėroximately 07:00 UTC each day.

All tips are apžvalgaed by an analyst before publishing. The automated layer identifies candidates; the human layer confirms them. If a tip is invalidated by late team naujass, it is replaced or withdrawn rather than left in place. Our tip evaluation framework tracks performance across a rolling 90-day window, and rinkas or leagues where our edge drops below a minimumas threshold are deprioritised until the model is recalibrated.
