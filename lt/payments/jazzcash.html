JazzCash indėlis at Stake for Pakistanasi žaidėjai

JazzCash, operated by Jazz (formerly Mobilink), is one of the two dominant mobilusis payment networks in Pakistanas and is pilnasy supported at Stake for PKR indėliss and išmokėjimass. Stake holds a Curaçao 1668/JAZ licence and has 400,000+ registruotised žaidėjai globally. JazzCash and Easypaisa together cover the vast majority of mobilusis money users in Pakistanas, giving Pakistanasi žaidėjai reliable local payment options. Enter reklamos kodas MAXBET at registracija to activate your sveikatos bonusasas on the pirmas indėlis.

How to indėlis with JazzCash at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and click Deposit. New users: registruotis pirmas and enter reklamos kodas MAXBET.
  2. Select JazzCash from the cashier. Enter the PKR indėlis amount (minimumas PKR 500). The screen shows a Stake JazzCash mobilusis sąskaita number.
  3. Open the JazzCash programėlė pirmame phone. Tap "Send Money" or "Mobile Account." Enter the Stake sąskaita number as the recipient.
  4. Confirm the amount and add your Stake user ID in the remarks field. Enter your JazzCash MPIN to authorise the transaction.
  5. Check your Stake balance. Funds programėlėear within 5 minutes. Note the JazzCash transaction ID from the programėlė confirmation screen.

How to withdraw from Stake to JazzCash

  1. Verify your identity on Stake. Submit CNIC details and a selfie in the Profile verification section before the pirmas išmokėjimas.
  2. Open Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose JazzCash.
  3. Enter your JazzCash registruotised number. Format: 03XX-XXXXXXX. This must be a number with an active JazzCash mobilusis sąskaita.
  4. Enter išmokėjimas amount and confirm details. The recipient name shown should match your Stake registracija name.
  5. Submit. Stake debits your wallet and dispatches the transfer. JazzCash credits your mobilusis sąskaita within 15 minutes to 3 hours. An SMS confirms receipt.

Limitai ir laikas

JazzCash mobilusis sąskaita limits depend pirmame KYC level. Level 0 (unverified, registruotised with mobilusis number only): maksimumas wallet balance PKR 25,000, daily transactions PKR 25,000. Level 1 (CNIC verified): maksimumas wallet balance PKR 200,000, daily transactions PKR 200,000. Level 2 (biometric verification at Jazz franchise): PKR 500,000 per month limit.

To maximise your indėlis and išmokėjimas limits at Stake via JazzCash, complete Level 1 verification by visiting any Jazz franchise with your CNIC. The process takes about 10 minutes and limits increase nedelsiant after biometric registracija.

JazzCash has 99%+ uptime and operates 24/7. Deposits are real-time confirmed. Withdrawals from Stake depend on the payment queue; most process within the pirmas hour during business hours (9 AM to 10 PM PKT).

Šalis availability and valiuta

JazzCash is prieinama to Pakistanasi residents with a Jazz or Warid SIM. As of 2024, JazzCash has over 25 million registruotised sąskaitas and 130,000+ franchise points across Pakistanas. All transactions are in PKR. JazzCash is regulated by the State Bank of Pakistanas (SBP) as a Branchless Banking licence holder.

Non-Jazz SIM holders can also registruotis for JazzCash using any Pakistanasi mobilusis number, though the wallet is primarily linked to Jazz/Warid numbers. Check JazzCash's website for non-Jazz registracija options.
