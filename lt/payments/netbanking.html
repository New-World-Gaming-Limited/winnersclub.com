How to indėlis via net banking at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Deposit" and select Net Banking from the payment options.
  2. Select your bank. A list of supported banks programėlėears. Choose your bank from the dropdown. If your bank is not listed, use UPI or IMPS as alternatives.
  3. Enter the indėlis amount. Input the INR amount (minimumas 500 INR). Confirm and click "Proceed to Bank."
  4. Log in pirmame bank's secure page. You are redirected to your bank's own net banking login. Enter your user ID and slaptažodis. Complete any additional verification (OTP, grid card, etc.) required by your bank.
  5. Authorise the payment. Confirm the transfer pirmame bank's portal. You are redirected back to Stake. The wallet updates within 5 to 120 minutes depending on whether the transfer routes via IMPS or NEFT.

How to withdraw to your bank sąskaita from Stake

  1. Complete KYC verification. Submit identity documents in your Stake profile before the pirmas išmokėjimas.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS).
  3. Enter your bank sąskaita details. Provide the sąskaita number, IFSC code, sąskaita holder name, and bank name.
  4. Enter the išmokėjimas amount. Confirm all details on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake debits your wallet nedelsiant and initiates the bank transfer. Funds arrive in 1 to 12 hours via NEFT or 30 minutes to 3 hours via IMPS. Your bank sends an SMS on credit.

Limitai ir laikas

Net banking indėlis limits at Stake are constrained by your bank's daily online transfer limit, which varies. SBI allows up to 10 lakh INR per day via net banking; HDFC and ICICI allow up to 25 lakh per day for retail customers. Check your bank's limit in your net banking profile under "Transaction Limits."

Stake's payment aggregator routes the transaction via IMPS for faster settlement or NEFT for larger amounts, depending on the aggregator's routing logic. In practice, most standard indėliss settle within 30 minutes during business hours.

Net banking requires an active internet connection and access to your bank's portal. If your bank enforces session time limits, complete the Stake payment page quickly. Sessions that expire mid-flow may result in a pending transaction that settles automatiškai within 30 minutes or returns to your bank sąskaita.

Šalis availability and valiuta

Net banking at Stake is prieinama for Indijan residents with sąskaitas at any major Indijan bank. All transactions are in INR. The list of supported banks through Stake's payment aggregator includes SBI, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Punjab National Bank, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, Union Bank of Indija, and many others.

If your bank is not in the supported list, use UPI or IMPS instead, as these payment rails cover all NPCI member banks regardless of net banking integration status.
