How to indėlis at Stake via Flutterwave

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: registruotis and enter reklamos kodas NEWBONUS.
  2. Select Flutterwave from the payment list. A Flutterwave checkout modal opens. Enter the indėlis amount in your local valiuta.
  3. Choose your preferred mokėjimo metodas inside Flutterwave checkout. Options typically include: Bank Transfer, Mobile Money (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Orange Money), Card (Visa/Mastercard), and USSD. Select the one that works best for you.
  4. Complete the payment in the Flutterwave flow. For mobilusis money: enter your phone number, confirm pirmame phone when prompted. For bank transfer: use the sąskaita number Flutterwave provides. For card: enter your card details.
  5. Return to Stake and check your balance. Mobile money and card indėliss update within 5 minutes. Bank transfers may take 15 to 60 minutes.

How to withdraw from Stake via Flutterwave

  1. Complete KYC on Stake. Submit a national ID and proof of address before the pirmas išmokėjimas.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Flutterwave.
  3. Select your išmokėjimas method. Bank sąskaita transfer or mobilusis money. Enter your bank sąskaita details or mobilusis money number.
  4. Enter the išmokėjimas amount. Confirm all details on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the išmokėjimas within 30 minutes to 24 hours depending on the method and country. Bank transfers may take longer due to interbank settlement times.

Limitai ir laikas

Flutterwave transaction limits vary by country, mokėjimo metodas, and merchant configuration. As a rule, mobilusis money options within Flutterwave inherit the limits of the underlying tiekėjas (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, etc.). Card transactions processed via Flutterwave are subject to 3D Secure verification and the issuing bank's international transaction limit.

Flutterwave processes over 200 million transactions per year across Africa and has 99.99% uptime. The system is engineered for the African banking environment, with automatic retries if a mobilusis money network experiences transient congestion.

For Nigeria specifically, Flutterwave supports Naira (NGN) bank transfers and card payments. Nigerian žaidėjai do not see mobilusis money in the Flutterwave flow as mobilusis money penetration via USSD is handled differently; instead, bank transfer and card are the primary options.

Flutterwave at Stake covers 34 countries including Nigeria, Gana, Kenija, Uganda, Tanzanija, Pietų Afrika, Rwanda, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Cameroon, Zambia, Ethiopia, Egypt, Morocco, and others. Each country is served in its local valiuta. Flutterwave is licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria, Bank of Gana, Bank of Tanzanija, Kenija's CBK, and 12 other African financial regulators.
