How to indėlis with Paytm at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake. New users: registruotis with reklamos kodas MAXBET to activate the sveikatos bonusasas.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Deposit. The mokėjimo metodas panel loads.
  3. Choose Paytm or UPI. Some versions of the Stake cashier show Paytm as a dedicated tile; others show a generic UPI input. Either will work with a Paytm VPA.
  4. Enter amount and complete payment. Enter 300 INR or more. A QR code or deep-link programėlėears. In the Paytm programėlė, tap "Scan and Pay" or follow the deep-link, verify the amount, and programėlėrove with your Paytm UPI PIN.
  5. Confirm the credit. The Stake wallet updates within 60 antrass. Save the Paytm Order ID from the programėlė in case you need to follow up.

How to withdraw to Paytm from Stake

  1. Complete identity verification. Upload an Aadhaar or PAN card scan in your Stake profile before requesting a išmokėjimas.
  2. Go to Cashier and select Withdrawal.
  3. Choose Paytm (UPI). Enter your Paytm VPA, typically mobilusisnumber@paytm.
  4. Specify the išmokėjimas amount. Verify the VPA on screen. Even a single digit error routes funds to the wrong sąskaita.
  5. Submit. Funds deduct from your wallet instantly and arrive in your Paytm-linked bank sąskaita within 15 minutes to 3 hours. A Paytm notification confirms receipt.

Limitai ir laikas

Paytm UPI transactions are subject to the same NPCI ceiling of 1,00,000 INR per transaction. Paytm's own daily transaction limit for pilnasy verified sąskaitas is also 1,00,000 INR. For the Paytm wallet (as opposed to UPI), limits are lower: 10,000 INR per transaction and 1,00,000 INR per month. Because Stake routes through UPI VPA, not the wallet balance, the higher limit programėlėlies.

During peak times like IPL season or major cricket matches, Paytm payment server load increases. If the payment takes more than 90 antrass to confirm, it is still being processed; do not restart the flow or you risk a duplicate transaction. Wait at least 3 minutes before concluding a payment has failed.

If your Paytm sąskaita is under Minimum KYC status, UPI limits may be lower than pilnas KYC sąskaitas. Upgrade to pilnas KYC in the Paytm programėlė for maksimumas limits.

Šalis availability and valiuta

Paytm is prieinama to Indijan residents only. Stake processes all Paytm transactions in INR. Paytm is regulated by the Reserve Bank of Indija (RBI) as a Payment Aggregator and must comply with RBI vadovaslines, which include transaction monitoring requirements.

Paytm has over 300 million registruotised users in Indija as of 2024. The UPI VPA linked to the Paytm programėlė can be associated with any Indijan bank, including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, and others.

The standard Paytm UPI handle is mobilusisnumber@paytm. This VPA draws from the bank sąskaita linked in the Paytm programėlė.
