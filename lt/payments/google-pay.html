How to indėlis using Google Pay at Stake

  1. Log in to your Stake sąskaita. New users: registruotis pirmas and enter reklamos kodas MAXBET.
  2. Click Deposit in the cashier. The mokėjimo metodas panel opens. Select Google Pay or the generic UPI option.
  3. Enter the indėlis amount. Minimum is 300 INR. The screen will display a QR code or UPI payment link.
  4. Open Google Pay pirmame phone. Tap "Scan QR code," point at the screen, or tap the UPI deep-link on mobilusis to jump directly into GPay. Confirm the amount and programėlėrove with your UPI PIN.
  5. Check your Stake wallet. Funds programėlėear within 60 antrass. If not, note the GPay transaction ID (visible in GPay under transaction history) and contact Stake tiesioginis-chat support.

How to withdraw to Google Pay from Stake

  1. Complete KYC. Submit a valid government-issued photo ID in your Stake profile settings before the pirmas išmokėjimas.
  2. Open the Withdrawal section of the cashier.
  3. Select Google Pay (UPI). Enter your GPay VPA. Standard formats: mobilusisnumber@okaxis, mobilusisnumber@okhdfcbank, mobilusisnumber@okicici, mobilusisnumber@oksbi.
  4. Enter the išmokėjimas amount. Double-check the VPA. An error in the VPA will cause the funds to reach the wrong sąskaita with no recourse.
  5. Submit. The wallet deducts nedelsiant. Funds arrive in 15 minutes to 3 hours; you will get a GPay notification on credit.

Limitai ir laikas

Google Pay on UPI inherits the NPCI per-transaction limit of 1,00,000 INR. Google also sets its own daily aggregate limit, which for pilnasy KYC-verified sąskaitas is 1,00,000 INR per day. Some banks that participate in GPay set lower limits; check your bank's UPI limit in your net banking portal if you experience declines at high amounts.

For indėliss, timing is effectively instant: the NPCI switch confirms transactions in real time. For išmokėjimass, Stake's payment processor queues and dispatches the request, which typically completes within the pirmas hour during the working day. Night-time išmokėjimass (midnight to 6 AM IST) may take slightly longer as the banking batch settlement runs.

No cap on the number of daily GPay indėliss from Stake's side, but if your bank declines after three failed attempts in a row, UPI may temporarily block further attempts to that VPA for up to 24 hours. In that case, use a different UPI programėlė or crypto.

Šalis availability and valiuta

Google Pay UPI is prieinama in Indija only. Stake processes GPay transactions exclusively in INR. If you have an overseas Google Pay sąskaita, it operates on different payment rails and is not the same product; Stake's UPI integration programėlėlies only to Indijan sąskaitas.

Google Pay is prieinama on Android and iOS. The programėlė requires an Indijan mobilusis number and an Indijan bank sąskaita to create a UPI VPA. As of 2024, Google Pay is the antras-largest UPI programėlė in Indija by transaction volume, ensuring high reliability and uptime.
