Most popular esporto lažybos rinkas

  • Match winner - Back one team to win the series (best-of-1, best-of-3, or best-of-5). The foundation rinka for all esportas titles on Stake.
  • Map winner (CS2/Valorant) - Bet on the winner of a specific map within a series. Because teams have known map preferences, map-winner rinkas offer strong value for those who study map pools.
  • Map handicap - In a best-of-3 series, backing the underdog at +1.5 maps means they need to win only one map. A common accumulator building block in CS2 and Valorant.
  • Total rounds (over/under) - In CS2, a map runs to 24 rounds maksimumas in regulation. Betting over or under a stated line (e.g. 25.5 rounds including overtime) rewards knowledge of individual team defensive and offensive styles.
  • First map winner - Which team wins Map 1? Often priced differently from the overall series winner if one side has a clear advantage on their chosen map but is weaker across the pilnas veto.
  • Correct score (series) - Predict the exact series scoreline: 2-0 or 2-1 in a best-of-3, or 3-0, 3-1, 3-2 in a best-of-5. Higher koeficientai reward precise forecasting.
  • Tournament outright - Back NaVi, T1, Team Spirit or any team to win the Major, The International or Worlds before the tournament begins for maksimumas koeficientai.
