Kaip veikia šis skaičiuotuvas

All lažybos koeficientai are just different ways of expressing the same underlying probability. The koeficientai converter takes any single format as input and derives a decimal representation pirmas, then converts outward to every other format using the exact mathematical relationships between them.

Decimal koeficientai represent iš viso return per unit staked, including your stake. An koeficientai of 2.50 means a 1-unit bet returns 2.50 units iš viso (1.50 profit). This is the native format at Stake and most European sporto knygas.

Fractional koeficientai are written as numerator/denominator (e.g. 6/4). Profit equals stake multiplied by numerator/denominator. A 6/4 bet on a 10-unit stake yields 15 units profit.

American (moneyline) koeficientai use positive numbers for underdogs and negative numbers for favorites. +150 means a 100-unit stake wins 150 profit. -200 means you need to stake 200 to win 100 profit.

Hong Kong koeficientai equal decimal koeficientai minus 1 (just the profit per unit). A decimal of 2.50 equals Hong Kong 1.50.

Indonesian koeficientai work like American but scaled per unit: positive values show profit per unit staked; negative values show how much you stake per unit of profit.

Malay koeficientai range from -1 to +1. Positive values represent profit per unit; negative values represent required stake per unit of profit. A Malay of -0.67 programėlėroximates American -150.

The implied probability formula is: probability = 1 / decimal koeficientai. A price of 2.50 carries 40% implied probability. Comparing this figure against your own assessed probability tells you whether a bet has positive expected value.

All conversions are lossless within floating-point precision. Fractional koeficientai are programėlėroximated to the nearest standard fraction when displaying (e.g. 1.5/1 rounds to 3/2), though the calculator uses the exact decimal internally for all outputs.

What is the most common koeficientai format?

Decimal koeficientai are the global standard used in Europe, Australia, and at Stake. Fractional koeficientai remain popular in the UK. American (moneyline) koeficientai dominate North American rinkas.

How do I convert decimal to fractional koeficientai?

Subtract 1 from the decimal, then express as a fraction in lowest terms. Decimal 2.50 becomes (2.50-1)/1 = 3/2 (written as 3/2 or "3 to 2").

What does implied probability mean?

Numanoma tikimybė is the percentage chance a bookmaker's price implies for an outcome. It equals 1 divided by the decimal koeficientai. If the rinka prices an outcome at 2.00, the implied probability is 50%.

How are American koeficientai converted to decimal?

For positive American koeficientai: decimal = (American / 100) + 1. For negative: decimal = (100 / |American|) + 1. So +200 = 3.00 decimal and -200 = 1.50 decimal.

What is the overround and why does it matter?

The overround (also called vig or juice) is the sum of all implied probabilities in a rinka minus 100%. A 5% overround means the book keeps roughly 5 cents per dollar wagered. Lower overround means better value for the bettor.
