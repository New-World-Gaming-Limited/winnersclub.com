How to deposit with Skrill at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. New users: register and enter کد تبلیغاتی MAXBET.
  2. Select Skrill from the payment method list. Enter the deposit amount in USD, EUR, or GBP.
  3. You are redirected to a Skrill login page. Log in to your Skrill account (or create one at skrill.com if you do not have one).
  4. Authorise the payment in Skrill. Confirm the amount and recipient (Stake). Skrill may send a one-time password (OTP) to your registered email or phone for 2FA confirmation.
  5. Return to Stake. The redirect brings you back to Stake automatically. Your wallet balance updates within 60 seconds. If it does not, check the Skrill transaction history and contact Stake live-chat with the Skrill transaction ID.

How to withdraw from Stake to Skrill

  1. Complete KYC on Stake. Upload a photo ID before the first withdrawal.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose Skrill.
  3. Enter your Skrill account email address. This is the email you use to log in to Skrill. Verify it carefully.
  4. Enter the withdrawal amount. Confirm the Skrill email on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake processes the withdrawal within 15 minutes to 3 hours. Once credited, funds appear instantly in your Skrill balance. You then withdraw from Skrill to your bank or card as needed.

For Stake deposits, the practical limit is whichever is lower: your Skrill account limit or Stake's own maximum.

Skrill deposits at Stake are instant, making it one of the best e-wallet options for time-sensitive deposits before a match starts. Withdrawals from Stake back to Skrill process within the hour for most requests during business hours.

FAQ: Skrill at Stake

What is the minimum Skrill deposit at Stake?

Minimum Skrill deposit at Stake is $10 USD (or equivalent in EUR or GBP). The exact minimum depends on your Skrill account currency.

How fast are Skrill deposits at Stake?

Skrill deposits are instant. Your Stake wallet updates within 60 seconds of completing payment in the Skrill flow.

Can I withdraw from Stake to Skrill?

Yes. Withdrawals to Skrill are supported. Processing takes 15 minutes to 3 hours from Stake's side; once credited to Skrill, funds are available instantly in your Skrill wallet.

Does Skrill charge fees for Stake transactions?

Skrill charges a currency conversion fee if your Skrill wallet currency differs from the transaction currency. Stake charges no additional fee. Skrill-to-bank withdrawal fees may apply when cashing out of Skrill.

My country is not supported by Skrill. What should I do?

Skrill has a restricted country list (some African and Asian countries). If Skrill is not available in your country, use a local mobile money method, Neteller, or crypto at Stake instead.
