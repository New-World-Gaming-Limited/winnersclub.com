Limiidid ja ajastus UPI jaoks Stake-is

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) caps individual UPI transactions at 1,00,000 INR (1 lakh). This is a hard ceiling rakenduslied at the NPCI infrastructure level, not a Stake policy. If you want to deposiit more in a session, place multiple transactions with a short gap between them.

There is no published weekly cap on UPI deposiits at Stake, but Stake's risk team may request additional identity checks if deposiit patterns spike unusually. On the väljamakse side, Stake processes requests in the order received. The esimene väljamakse of a calendar month may take slightly longer (up to 4 hours) because the compliance team performs a routine konto arvustus on esimene väljamakses.

Timing note: UPI payments are 24/7, including bank holidays and weekends, because NPCI operates around the clock. The 15 minute to 3 hour väljamakse window is the standard range; most complete in under an hour during business hours.
