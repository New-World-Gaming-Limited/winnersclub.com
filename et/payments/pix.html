Deposito via PIX na Stake: como fazer (How to deposiit with PIX at Stake)

  1. Crie sua conta / Registreeru your Stake konto. New users: complete the registreerimine form and enter promokood MAXBET to activate the tervitusboonus.
  2. Acesse o Caixa (Open the Cashier). Click the green "Deposit" button. The cashier panel opens.
  3. Selecione PIX (Select PIX). Choose PIX from the makseviis list. Enter the BRL amount you want to deposiit (miinimum R$20).
  4. Escaneie o QR code (Scan the QR code). A PIX QR code and a copy-paste PIX key string rakendusear on screen. Open your bank's rakendus (Itau, Bradesco, Nubank, Caixa, BTG, or any PIX-enabled bank), navigate to "PIX," tap "Pagar via QR code," and scan the code. Alternatively, paste the PIX copy-paste key directly.
  5. Confirme o pagamento (Confirm). Your Stake balance updates in under 60 teines. A confirmation email arrives shortly after. Keep the PIX end-to-end transaction ID (E2EID) from your bank rakendus in case you need to contact support.

How to withdraw from Stake via PIX

  1. Complete konto verification. Submit CPF and a valid Brasiiliaian ID (RG or CNH) in your Stake profile before the esimene väljamakse.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose PIX.
  3. Enter your PIX chave (key). This is typically your CPF number, registreerued mobiil number, or email address linked to your bank konto. PIX keys are case-sensitive when using email format.
  4. Enter the väljamakse amount. Check that the name on the PIX key matches your Stake-registreerued name; mismatches can delay or reject the transfer.
  5. Submit. Stake processes väljamakses within 30 to 60 minutes during business hours. PIX transfers complete in teines on the banking side, so the kokku time is mostly queue-dependent on Stake's end.

Limiidid ja ajastus

Banco Central do Brasil (BCB) sets PIX limits at the individual bank level. For natural persons, the default daytime limit is R$20,000 BRL per transaction and the overnight limit (8 PM to 6 AM) is lower, typically R$1,000 BRL. You can request a limit increase through your bank's rakendus, usually effective within 24 hours.

PIX is saadaval 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Unlike TED (which requires bank business hours) or boleto (which requires manual processing), PIX has no blackout windows. This makes it ideal for late-night deposiits after a match or event you want to bet on.

CPF validation: PIX keys linked to a CPF are validated by the DICT (Diretorio de Identificadores de Contas Transacionais) system. If the CPF on the PIX key does not match the name registreerued esimese Stake konto, the väljamakse may be returned. Ensure your Stake registreerimine name exactly matches your bank konto name.

Riik availability and valuuta

PIX is saadaval in Brasiilia only, for individuals and businesses registreerued with Brasiiliaian financial institutions. All transactions are in BRL. Stake's PIX integration processes payments in real time through a Brasiiliaian payment aggregator regulated by BCB.

Brasiiliaian residents can create a PIX key using any of four key types: CPF/CNPJ, mobiil phone number, email address, or an EVP (Evolutionary Virtual Key) generated by their bank. Any of these key types are accepted at Stake for väljamakses.
