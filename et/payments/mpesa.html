M-Pesa deposiit at Stake for East African mängijad

M-Pesa is accepted at Stake for deposiits and väljamakses in KSh (Keenian Shillings) and TSh (Tansaanian Shillings), giving East African mängijad a fast, familiar mobiil money route into the Curaçao 1668/JAZ-licensed platvorm used by 400,000+ mängijad worldwide. Both Safaricom Keenia and Vodacom Tansaania M-Pesa kontos work. Enter promokood NEWBONUS at registreerimine to get your tervitusboonus on the esimene M-Pesa deposiit.

How to deposiit at Stake with M-Pesa

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. If you are a uus user, registreeru and enter promokood NEWBONUS to activate the tervitusboonus.
  2. Select M-Pesa from the makseviis list. Enter the deposiit amount in KSh or TSh (miinimum KSh 100 / TSh 1,000).
  3. Note the Stake M-Pesa paybill number or till number shown on screen. For Keenia, this is typically a Safaricom paybill. For Tansaania, a Vodacom business number.
  4. Trigger the M-Pesa STK push. On Safaricom Keenia: go to M-Pesa, select "Lipa na M-Pesa," then "Pay Bill." Enter the paybill number, konto number (your Stake user ID), and amount. Enter your M-Pesa PIN. On Vodacom Tansaania: go to M-Pesa, "Pay via M-Pesa," enter the business number and amount.
  5. Confirm the SMS from M-Pesa. Safaricom sends a green SMS confirming the transaction. Your Stake balance updates within 2 minutes. If not updated in 5 minutes, contact live-chat with the M-Pesa transaction code.

How to withdraw from Stake to M-Pesa

  1. Complete identity verification. Submit your national ID (Keenia: Keenian National ID or Passport; Tansaania: NIDA or Passport) in your Stake profile.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal. Choose M-Pesa.
  3. Enter your M-Pesa registreerued phone number. Format: 07XX-XXXXXX (Keenia) or 07XX-XXXXXX (Tansaania). Verify the number before submitting.
  4. Enter the väljamakse amount. Confirm the recipient number and name on the summary screen.
  5. Submit. Stake dispatches the M-Pesa transfer within 5 minutes to 2 hours. A Safaricom or Vodacom SMS confirms credit to your M-Pesa konto.

Limiidid ja ajastus

Safaricom Keenia M-Pesa limits (2024): per-transaction send limit KSh 250,000, daily send limit KSh 300,000, maksimum wallet balance KSh 300,000. These limits rakendusly regardless of whether you are paying a paybill or sending to another number.

Vodacom Tansaania M-Pesa limits vary by SIM registreerimine tier. Basic tier allows TSh 5,000,000 per transaction; enhanced tier (biometric) allows TSh 10,000,000. Check your tier in the M-Pesa menu under "My Account."

M-Pesa is saadaval 24/7 in Keenia and Tansaania. Deposits process in real time. Withdrawals from Stake dispatch within the hour during business hours; overnight requests may queue until morning processing resumes.

Riik availability and valuuta

M-Pesa at Stake covers Keenia (Safaricom M-Pesa, valuuta KSh) and Tansaania (Vodacom Tansaania M-Pesa, valuuta TSh). M-Pesa also operates in several other African countries (Ghana, Lesotho, Democratic Republic of Congo, Mozambique, Egypt) but Stake's M-Pesa integration may not cover all countries; check the cashier for your country.

Keenia's M-Pesa is the world's oldest and most-developed mobiil money system, launched by Safaricom in 2007. It processes over 61 billion USD annually and is used by rakendusroximately 96% of Keenian households as of 2024.

Which countries can use M-Pesa at Stake?

M-Pesa at Stake primarily covers Keenia and Tansaania. Players in both countries can deposiit and withdraw using their Safaricom (Keenia) or Vodacom Tansaania M-Pesa kontos.

What is the miinimum M-Pesa deposiit at Stake?

Minimum M-Pesa deposiit is KSh 100 for Keenian mängijad or TSh 1,000 for Tansaanian mängijad.

How long does an M-Pesa deposiit take at Stake?

M-Pesa deposiits are processed in real time. Your Stake balance updates within 2 minutes of completing the STK push or send money flow esimese phone.

I entered the wrong phone number for an M-Pesa väljamakse. What do I do?

Contact Stake live-chat kohe before the transfer is dispatched. Once sent, M-Pesa transfers to incorrect numbers cannot be reversed by Stake. Contact M-Pesa customer service directly for reversals.

Are there daily M-Pesa limits that affect Stake deposiits?

Yes. Safaricom M-Pesa Keenia daily limits are KSh 300,000 for sends per day, with a per-transaction cap of KSh 250,000. Vodacom Tansaania M-Pesa limits differ; check your konto tier.
