How to deposiit with Boleto at Stake

  1. Log in to Stake and open the Cashier. Click "Deposit" and select Boleto from the makseviis list.
  2. Enter the deposiit amount. Input the BRL amount (miinimum R$30). A boleto PDF or barcode is generated on screen.
  3. Save or print the boleto. The boleto has a barcode and a numeric string (linha digitavel). Note the due date; boletos typically expire within 1 to 3 days.
  4. Pay the boleto. You can pay via your bank's rakendus (scan the barcode), Loteria (lottery kiosk), ATM, or any bank branch. Payment via banking rakendus is fastest. Enter the barcode number or scan the QR code if your bank supports boleto QR.
  5. Wait for processing. After payment, boleto takes 1 to 3 business days to clear. Your Stake balance updates once the bank confirms settlement. If you need funds credited faster for an upcoming event, use PIX instead.

How to withdraw from Stake as a Brasiiliaian player (not via Boleto)

  1. Boleto is deposiit-only. For väljamakses, use PIX via the cashier väljamakse section.
  2. Open the Cashier and select Withdrawal.
  3. Choose PIX. Enter your CPF-linked PIX key or phone number.
  4. Enter the väljamakse amount. Confirm the PIX key details match your registreerued name.
  5. Submit. PIX väljamakses process within 30 to 60 minutes from Stake's side; the actual bank transfer completes in teines.

Limiidid ja ajastus

Boleto processing is not instant. After you pay, the clearing house (CIP or Febraban) processes the payment, which takes 1 to 2 business days at miinimum. Weekend payments (Saturday or Sunday) typically do not settle until Monday or Tuesday. Public holidays add one business day each.

For time-sensitive deposiits, PIX is the clear superior option. Boleto is best used when you plan ahead and want to fund your Stake konto days before an event or tournament.

The boleto face value must exactly match the amount entered in the Stake cashier. If you pay a different amount (e.g., rounding up), the entire payment may be rejected and returned to you after 3 to 5 business days.

Maximum boleto values vary by the issuing bank. Most Stake boletos cap at R$10,000 BRL per slip. For larger deposiits, generate multiple boletos or use PIX or crypto.
